This Quinoa and Black Bean Salad is a refreshing, protein-packed dish that’s as nutritious as it is delicious. Fluffy quinoa forms the base, combined with hearty black beans, sweet corn, cherry tomatoes, and crisp bell peppers for a medley of textures and colors. A tangy lime-cilantro dressing ties the ingredients together, adding a zesty kick and fresh herbal notes. Packed with fiber, plant-based protein, and essential nutrients, this salad is perfect as a light lunch, a side dish, or a potluck favorite. Easy to prepare and naturally gluten-free, it’s a versatile recipe that can be customized with your favorite toppings, like avocado, sliced jalapeños, or a sprinkle of pumpkin seeds. Serve chilled or at room temperature for a vibrant and satisfying meal.
