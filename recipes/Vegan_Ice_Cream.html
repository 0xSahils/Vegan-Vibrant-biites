Vegan Ice Cream

Dive into a bowl of creamy, dreamy Vegan Ice Cream that’s as rich and indulgent as the traditional kind—without any dairy! Made with wholesome plant-based ingredients like coconut milk, almond milk, or cashew cream, this vegan treat is naturally creamy and full of flavor. Customize your ice cream with a variety of flavors, from classic vanilla and decadent chocolate to fruity strawberry or mango. Sweetened with natural options like maple syrup, agave, or dates, it’s a healthier alternative that doesn’t compromise on taste. Perfect for cooling off on a hot day or as a satisfying dessert, Vegan Ice Cream can be made at home with simple ingredients and an ice cream maker—or even without one! Whether you’re topping it with fresh fruit, crushed nuts, or a drizzle of vegan chocolate syrup, each bite is a celebration of flavor and texture. This guilt-free dessert is loved by vegans and non-vegans alike. Whether you’re hosting a gathering or enjoying a quiet treat, Vegan Ice Cream is the perfect way to indulge your sweet tooth while staying true to your plant-based lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup or agave syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Optional mix-ins: chocolate chips, crushed nuts, fresh fruit

Instructions

  1. Mix the ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth.
  2. Chill the mixture: Place the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until well-chilled.
  3. Churn the ice cream: If you have an ice cream maker, pour the chilled mixture into the machine and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions. If you don't have an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into a shallow dish and place it in the freezer.
  4. Freeze: If you're using an ice cream maker, transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm. If you're not using an ice cream maker, stir the mixture every 30 minutes for about 3 hours to prevent ice crystals from forming.
  5. Serve: Once the ice cream is firm, scoop and serve with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, nuts, or chocolate chips.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 220
  • Protein: 2g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 1g
  • Sodium: 45mg
