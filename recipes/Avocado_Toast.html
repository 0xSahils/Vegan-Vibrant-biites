Avocado Toast

Avocado toast is the ultimate quick, healthy, and delicious breakfast or snack that never goes out of style. It's a simple yet flavorful dish featuring creamy, ripe avocados spread generously over crunchy, toasted bread. This vegan-friendly delight is rich in healthy fats, fiber, and nutrients, making it both nourishing and satisfying. The base of the dish is a slice of toasted breadâ€”choose sourdough, multigrain, or gluten-free options for variety. The star ingredient, avocado, is mashed to a creamy consistency and often enhanced with a pinch of salt, black pepper, and a squeeze of fresh lemon or lime juice for a zesty kick.

Avocado toast can be customized endlessly to suit your taste preferences. Top it with fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley, sprinkle red chili flakes for a spicy kick, or drizzle olive oil for added richness. For extra crunch, add seeds like sesame, chia, or pumpkin. You can also layer on thinly sliced vegetables like cucumbers, radishes, or cherry tomatoes, or give it a tangy twist with pickled onions.

For a hearty version, consider adding plant-based protein like chickpeas, roasted tofu, or a sprinkle of nutritional yeast. Whether you're keeping it simple or getting creative, avocado toast is always a crowd-pleaser, offering a perfect blend of flavors and textures in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread (or can choose any bread of desire. Best will be home made bread with reditional method)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Optional: Cherry tomatoes, chili flakes, or lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Toast the Bread: Place your bread slices in a toaster or on a skillet until they are golden and crispy.
  2. Prepare the Avocado: Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash it with a fork until smooth.
  3. Season the Avocado: Add a pinch of salt and pepper to the mashed avocado. Mix well.
  4. Assemble the Toast: Spread the seasoned avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices.
  5. Add Toppings: If desired, top with cherry tomatoes, chili flakes, or a squeeze of lemon juice for added flavor.
  6. Serve: Enjoy immediately as a quick, healthy meal or snack.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 6g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Fiber: 7g

  • Potassium: 10% of Daily Value
