Vegan Pasta

This vegan pasta is a mouthwatering dish that combines a flavorful tomato-based sauce with a variety of fresh vegetables, making it a nutrient-packed meal. The combination of tender pasta, aromatic garlic, and sautéed onion provides the base for this comforting dish. Bell peppers and zucchini add a burst of color and texture, while herbs like oregano and basil bring an authentic Italian flavor. Whether you’re preparing a quick weeknight dinner or a more elaborate meal for friends and family, this recipe is sure to impress. Easy to make, healthy, and customizable, this vegan pasta is a must-try for anyone looking to explore delicious plant-based options.

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Optional: Red chili flakes for a spicy kick

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Drain and set aside, reserving a cup of pasta water.
  2. Prepare the aromatics: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion, cooking until they become fragrant and translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Sauté the vegetables: Add the diced bell pepper and sliced zucchini to the skillet. Stir well and cook for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still slightly crisp.
  4. Create the sauce: Pour in the canned crushed tomatoes and tomato paste. Stir to combine. Add the dried oregano, basil, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Adjust consistency: If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of reserved pasta water to achieve the desired consistency. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  6. Combine and serve: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss gently to coat each strand with the sauce. Let the pasta sit in the sauce for 2 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  7. Plate and garnish: Transfer the pasta to serving plates and garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Add red chili flakes if desired. Serve warm and enjoy your vegan pasta!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 55g
  • Fat: 8g
  • Fiber: 7g
  • Sodium: 400mg
  • Vitamin C: 35% DV
