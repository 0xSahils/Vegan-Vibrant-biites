Maple Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Maple Roasted Brussels Sprouts are a delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors, making them a standout side dish for any meal. The Brussels sprouts are roasted to perfection, creating a crispy, golden-brown exterior while maintaining a tender interior. The addition of maple syrup adds a natural sweetness that enhances the earthy flavor of the sprouts, creating a beautifully caramelized glaze that’s both delicious and visually appealing. A hint of salt and optional spices like black pepper or a dash of chili flakes provide the perfect balance, making every bite a burst of flavor. This dish is versatile enough to be served at holiday gatherings, alongside a hearty main course, or as a simple yet elegant addition to a weeknight dinner. The roasting process not only brings out the natural sweetness of the Brussels sprouts but also gives them a satisfying crunch that even skeptics will love. For added texture and flavor, you can sprinkle toasted nuts or dried cranberries before serving. Maple Roasted Brussels Sprouts are a wholesome, crowd-pleasing dish that will elevate any meal, leaving everyone at the table coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved: Fresh Brussels sprouts for roasting.
  • 2 tbsp olive oil: For roasting the Brussels sprouts and enhancing flavor.
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup: For a natural sweet glaze.
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar: Adds a touch of tanginess to balance the sweetness.
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder: For a savory note.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the Brussels sprouts: Trim the ends of the Brussels sprouts and slice them in half.
  3. Toss with oil and seasonings: In a large bowl, toss the Brussels sprouts with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Roast the Brussels sprouts: Spread the Brussels sprouts on a baking sheet in a single layer. Roast for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until they are golden brown and crispy.
  5. Glaze with maple syrup: Drizzle maple syrup and balsamic vinegar over the roasted Brussels sprouts, and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Serve: Serve hot as a delicious side dish to any meal. Enjoy!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 130
  • Protein: 4g
  • Carbohydrates: 18g
  • Fat: 6g
  • Fiber: 6g
