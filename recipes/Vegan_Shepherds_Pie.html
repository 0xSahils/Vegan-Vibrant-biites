Vegan Shepherd's Pie

Enjoy the comforting and hearty flavors of Vegan Shepherd’s Pie, a plant-based twist on a classic comfort dish. This recipe features a rich and savory filling made with lentils, mushrooms, carrots, peas, and onions, all simmered in a flavorful gravy. Topped with a creamy layer of mashed potatoes, it’s baked to golden perfection for a dish that’s both satisfying and nourishing.

Perfect for cozy dinners or special occasions, Vegan Shepherd’s Pie is a crowd-pleaser that everyone at the table will enjoy. It’s packed with protein and fiber, making it a wholesome option for plant-based eaters and beyond. The combination of tender vegetables and the creamy potato topping creates a delightful contrast of textures and flavors in every bite.

This dish is incredibly versatile and can be adapted to suit your preferences. You can add seasonal vegetables, swap out the lentils for chickpeas, or even use sweet potatoes for a twist on the traditional topping. Serve it with a simple green salad or crusty bread to complete the meal.

Vegan Shepherd’s Pie is a comforting classic reimagined, delivering all the warmth and flavor of the original without any animal products. It’s a recipe you’ll want to make again and again!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup peas (frozen or fresh)
  • 1 cup lentils, cooked
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups vegetable broth (How to make Vegan Vegetable Broth here).
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup plant-based milk
  • 2 tbsp vegan butter
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Filling: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the diced carrot and cook for another 3-4 minutes until slightly tender.
  2. Add Lentils and Peas: Stir in the cooked lentils, peas, tomato paste, vegetable broth, thyme, rosemary, and soy sauce. Simmer the mixture for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine and the sauce to thicken. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Prepare the Mashed Potatoes: While the filling is simmering, place the peeled and cubed potatoes in a pot of salted water. Bring to a boil and cook until the potatoes are fork-tender, about 15 minutes. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Mash the potatoes with plant-based milk and vegan butter until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Assemble the Shepherd's Pie: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Spread the lentil and vegetable filling evenly in the bottom of a baking dish. Top with a generous layer of mashed potatoes, spreading it out evenly with a spatula.
  5. Bake: Bake the Shepherd's Pie in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown. For a crispier top, broil for an additional 3-5 minutes.
  6. Serve: Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired, and serve warm.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 400
  • Protein: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 12g
  • Sodium: 500mg
