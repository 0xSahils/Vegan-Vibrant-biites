Vegan Zucchini Noodle Pesto

These Vegan Zucchini Noodles with Pesto are a light and flavorful alternative to traditional pasta, perfect for a healthy and satisfying meal. Fresh zucchini noodles, or zoodles, are paired with a creamy and herbaceous vegan pesto made from basil, garlic, nutritional yeast, and nuts or seeds. The dish is as delicious as it is versatile. The pesto, bursting with vibrant flavors, clings beautifully to the spiralized zucchini, creating a meal that’s low in carbs yet high in taste. You can customize the recipe by adding cherry tomatoes, sautéed mushrooms, or crispy chickpeas for added texture and nutrition. Ready in under 20 minutes, these zucchini noodles are ideal for a quick weeknight dinner or a light lunch. Serve them warm or chilled, making them perfect for any season. They’re naturally gluten-free, dairy-free, and packed with nutrients, making this dish a great choice for anyone following a plant-based or health-conscious lifestyle. Whether you’re looking to include more veggies in your diet or just want to try a fresh and tasty twist on pasta night, these Vegan Zucchini Noodles with Pesto are sure to impress. Enjoy them as a main course or a side dish that’s both delicious and guilt-free.

Ingredients

  • 2 large zucchinis (about 2 cups of zucchini noodles), spiralized into noodles: Fresh, firm zucchinis work best for spiralizing. They provide a crisp texture and are low in calories, making them an excellent substitute for traditional pasta in this dish.
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves: Fresh basil is key to making a fragrant, aromatic pesto. It gives the sauce a vibrant green color and a burst of flavor. For a more intense basil taste, consider using organic basil.
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts: Pine nuts add a rich, buttery flavor and help to create the smooth texture of the pesto sauce. You can toast them lightly for a deeper flavor profile.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Garlic adds a pungent, savory depth to the pesto. Fresh garlic is best for the most aromatic and flavorful result.
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast: Nutritional yeast contributes a cheesy, nutty flavor, perfect for vegan dishes. It also adds a boost of B vitamins, making it a nutritious alternative to Parmesan.
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil: Olive oil is the base for the pesto sauce, providing a smooth and velvety consistency. Choose a high-quality, cold-pressed olive oil for the best flavor.
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (freshly squeezed): Lemon juice adds a refreshing acidity to balance the rich, creamy pesto. Fresh lemon juice enhances the brightness of the dish, giving it a fresh, zesty finish.
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt: Salt enhances all the flavors in the pesto and brings out the natural sweetness of the zucchini noodles. Adjust the amount to taste.
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper (freshly ground): Black pepper adds a subtle heat and depth to the pesto. Freshly ground pepper is always more flavorful and aromatic.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pesto: In a food processor or blender, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pulse until finely chopped.
  2. Blend the pesto: With the processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the pesto reaches a creamy consistency. Scrape down the sides of the bowl as needed. Taste and adjust seasoning as desired.
  3. Prepare the zucchini noodles: Heat a large pan over medium heat and add the zucchini noodles. Sauté for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the noodles are tender but still firm.
  4. Combine the noodles and pesto: Toss the zucchini noodles with the pesto sauce until evenly coated. Serve immediately, garnished with additional pine nuts or fresh basil if desired.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 7g
  • Carbohydrates: 18g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Fiber: 4g
