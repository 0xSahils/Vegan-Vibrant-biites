Stuffed Zucchini Boats

These Stuffed Zucchini Boats are a delightful combination of flavor and nutrition, making them a perfect choice for a wholesome meal. Each zucchini is carefully hollowed out and filled with a savory mixture of quinoa, fresh vegetables, and aromatic herbs, creating a satisfying balance of textures and flavors. The natural sweetness of zucchini pairs beautifully with the hearty filling, while the baking process enhances the overall taste, giving the zucchini a tender yet slightly firm texture. This dish is not only vegan but also packed with essential nutrients like fiber, plant-based protein, and a variety of vitamins and minerals. It's an excellent choice for anyone looking to enjoy a healthy, balanced meal that's light yet fulfilling. These zucchini boats are versatile, easy to prepare, and perfect for serving as a main course for lunch or dinner. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or simply cooking for your family, this recipe is sure to impress with its vibrant presentation and irresistible flavors. Pair it with a fresh side salad or some crusty bread for an even more satisfying dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis: Halved lengthwise and hollowed out.
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa: This serves as the base for the stuffing.
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper: Adds sweetness and crunch.
  • 1/2 cup diced onion: Adds flavor and texture.
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes: Adds juiciness to the stuffing.
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil: Adds freshness and flavor.
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast: For a cheesy, vegan flavor.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing the vegetables.
  • 1 tsp garlic powder: Adds depth of flavor.
  • 1 tsp onion powder: For extra seasoning.
  • 1/2 tsp salt: To taste.
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper: For seasoning.
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika: Adds a smoky flavor (optional).
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice: For added brightness.
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs: For topping the stuffed zucchinis for a crispy finish.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the zucchini: Cut the zucchinis in half lengthwise and scoop out the insides using a spoon to create a boat shape. Set aside the zucchini pulp for later use.
  3. Cook the vegetables: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the onion, bell pepper, and tomatoes. Sauté for about 5-7 minutes, or until softened.
  4. Prepare the stuffing: In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, sautéed vegetables, zucchini pulp, basil, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, paprika, and lemon juice. Mix until well combined.
  5. Stuff the zucchinis: Fill each zucchini half with the quinoa mixture, pressing down gently to pack it in. Place the stuffed zucchini boats on a baking sheet.
  6. Top with breadcrumbs: Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top of each stuffed zucchini for a crispy finish.
  7. Bake: Bake the stuffed zucchini boats for 20-25 minutes, or until the zucchini is tender and the topping is golden brown.
  8. Serve: Remove from the oven and serve immediately, garnished with fresh basil if desired. Enjoy your stuffed zucchini boats!
