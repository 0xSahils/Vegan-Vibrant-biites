Vegan Burgers

These vegan burgers are an incredible plant-based alternative that is hearty, flavorful, and incredibly satisfying. Made with wholesome ingredients like black beans, oats, and a blend of spices, these burgers hold together beautifully and are perfect for grilling or pan-frying. Topped with fresh vegetables, creamy avocado, and your favorite vegan condiments, these burgers are sure to be a hit at any meal. Whether you're hosting a BBQ, looking for a quick weeknight dinner, or simply craving a delicious and nutritious burger, this recipe has you covered. Customize it with your favorite toppings and enjoy a healthy, meat-free option that's bursting with taste.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot (optional)
  • 1 small green chili, finely chopped (optional for spice)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for cooking)
  • Burger buns, lettuce, tomato slices, and onion rings (for serving)
  • Optional: Vegan mayo, mustard, pickles, or your favorite condiments

Instructions

  1. Prepare the potatoes: Peel and boil the potatoes until fork-tender. Drain and mash them in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Mix the ingredients: Add breadcrumbs, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, grated carrot, green chilies (if using), chopped cilantro, salt, pepper, cumin, smoked paprika, and a dash of lemon juice. Mix well to form a thick dough-like consistency.
  3. Shape the patties: Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Refrigerate for 20-30 minutes to help them firm up.
  4. Cook the patties: Heat a pan with a little oil over medium heat. Cook each patty for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Assemble the burger: Toast the burger buns if desired. Layer each bun with lettuce, a potato patty, tomato slices, onion rings, and vegan mayo or your favorite sauce. Top with the other half of the bun.
  6. Serve and enjoy: Serve the burgers warm with a side of fries or a fresh salad. Enjoy your crispy and flavorful vegan potato patty burger!
