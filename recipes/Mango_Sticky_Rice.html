Vegan Mango Sticky Rice

This Vegan Mango Sticky Rice is a luscious dessert that combines the fragrant sweetness of ripe mangoes with the creamy richness of coconut milk-infused sticky rice. Inspired by Thai cuisine, this dish offers a delightful balance of flavors and textures, perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth. The tender, slightly chewy rice is elevated by the natural tropical aroma of coconut milk, while the juicy mango slices add a refreshing contrast. Whether served at a festive gathering or enjoyed as an indulgent treat, this simple yet elegant dessert is sure to impress and leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sticky rice (glutinous rice): Rinsed and soaked for 4-6 hours or overnight.
  • 1 1/4 cups coconut milk: Full-fat coconut milk for creaminess.
  • 1/4 cup sugar: Adjust to taste, preferably cane or coconut sugar.
  • 1/4 tsp salt: To balance the sweetness.
  • 2 ripe mangoes: Peeled, pitted, and sliced into thin strips.
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds: For garnish (optional).
  • 1-2 tbsp coconut flakes: For garnish (optional).

Instructions

  1. Cook the sticky rice: Rinse the sticky rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 4-6 hours or overnight. Steam the rice in a bamboo or metal steamer for 20-30 minutes until tender.
  2. Prepare the coconut milk sauce: In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Cook over medium heat until the sugar dissolves, stirring occasionally. Do not boil.
  3. Combine rice and coconut milk: Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl. Pour the coconut milk sauce over the rice and mix gently. Let it sit for about 10-15 minutes to absorb the coconut milk flavor.
  4. Prepare the mango: Peel and slice the mangoes into thin strips.
  5. Assemble the dish: Serve the coconut sticky rice in bowls, topped with fresh mango slices. Garnish with sesame seeds and coconut flakes if desired.
