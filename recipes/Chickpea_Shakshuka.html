Chickpea Shakshuka

This Chickpea Shakshuka is a hearty, flavorful dish that brings a delightful twist to a traditional favorite. Made with a rich, spiced tomato sauce simmered to perfection, it features tender chickpeas that add a satisfying texture and boost of protein. Infused with aromatic spices like cumin, paprika, and chili flakes, this dish offers a harmonious balance of warmth and zest. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a light dinner, this vegan take on shakshuka is as versatile as it is delicious. Serve it with crusty bread for scooping up every last bit of the vibrant sauce, or pair it with a side salad for a complete meal. Whether you’re hosting a weekend brunch or simply craving a comforting dish, this Chickpea Shakshuka is sure to impress with its bold flavors and wholesome ingredients. It's easy to make, packed with nutrients, and a true crowd-pleaser for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing.
  • 1 onion: Finely chopped.
  • 3 garlic cloves: Minced.
  • 1 red bell pepper: Diced.
  • 1 tsp ground cumin: Adds depth of flavor.
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika: For a smoky kick.
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes: Optional, for heat.
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes: Base for the sauce.
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas: Drained and rinsed.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • Fresh parsley , avocado sliced(optional): Chopped, for garnish .
  • Pita bread or crusty bread: For serving.

Instructions

  1. Heat oil: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté vegetables: Add onions, garlic, and red bell pepper. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add spices: Stir in cumin, smoked paprika, and red pepper flakes. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomatoes: Pour in crushed tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add chickpeas: Stir in chickpeas and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for another 5-7 minutes.
  6. Garnish and serve: Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve warm with pita bread or crusty bread.
