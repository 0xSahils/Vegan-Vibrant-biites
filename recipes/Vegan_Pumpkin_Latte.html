Vegan Pumpkin Latte

Cozy up with a warm and comforting Vegan Pumpkin Latte, the perfect autumn treat. Made with creamy plant-based milk, real pumpkin puree, and a delightful blend of spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves, this latte captures the essence of fall in every sip. Sweetened with natural sweeteners like maple syrup or agave, it’s a healthier alternative to traditional pumpkin lattes while still delivering the rich, indulgent flavor you crave. This recipe is quick and easy, allowing you to enjoy a café-quality beverage in the comfort of your own home. Top it off with a dollop of vegan whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon for an extra touch of decadence. Perfect for crisp mornings or chilly evenings, this Vegan Pumpkin Latte is not only delicious but also packed with the goodness of pumpkin, which is rich in vitamins and antioxidants. Whether you’re a pumpkin spice enthusiast or simply looking for a warm drink to elevate your day, this latte is sure to become a seasonal favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup canned pumpkin puree: The base of the pumpkin flavor.
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk): For a creamy consistency.
  • 1 shot of espresso or 1/2 cup brewed coffee: For the rich coffee flavor.
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup: Adds natural sweetness.
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon: For warmth and spice.
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg: Adds a subtle, earthy flavor.
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger: For a touch of heat and flavor depth.
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves (optional): Adds a deeper fall flavor.
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract: For a hint of sweetness.
  • Pinch of sea salt: To balance the sweetness and spice.
  • Whipped coconut cream (optional): For a creamy topping.

Instructions

  1. Brew the coffee: If you’re using espresso, brew 1 shot. If using brewed coffee, brew 1/2 cup.
  2. Make the pumpkin spice mix: In a small saucepan, combine the pumpkin puree, almond milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, cloves (if using), vanilla extract, and sea salt. Heat over medium heat, whisking occasionally, until it’s hot and well combined.
  3. Sweeten the latte: Add maple syrup to the pumpkin mixture and stir until dissolved. Adjust the sweetness to your liking.
  4. Combine with coffee: Pour the brewed coffee or espresso into your mug. Then, pour the pumpkin spice mixture over the coffee and stir well.
  5. Optional toppings: Top with a dollop of whipped coconut cream for an extra indulgent treat.
  6. Serve: Enjoy your Vegan Pumpkin Latte hot, or let it cool down for an iced version!
