Vegan Snickerdoodle Cookies

These Vegan Snickerdoodle Cookies are a cozy, sweet treat that combines the perfect balance of cinnamon and sugar. Soft and chewy with a slight crunch on the outside, they’re a delightful twist on the traditional snickerdoodle cookie, made entirely plant-based. The warm, comforting flavor of cinnamon is complemented by a subtle sweetness, making them a perfect addition to any gathering or a simple snack to enjoy with a cup of tea or coffee. The dough is made using simple vegan ingredients like flour, coconut oil, and plant-based milk, making it easy to prepare and enjoy. Whether you’re baking for the holidays, a special occasion, or just a sweet indulgence, these cookies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. The cinnamon-sugar coating gives them a signature snickerdoodle flavor, while the vegan twist makes them suitable for those with dietary preferences or restrictions. Plus, these cookies are a hit for both vegans and non-vegans alike, with everyone loving their soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture. You can also make them ahead of time and store them for up to a week, ensuring you have a delightful treat on hand whenever cravings strike. They are also easily customizable, so feel free to add in your favorite mix-ins, like vegan chocolate chips or chopped nuts, for an extra twist!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour: The base of the dough, providing that classic cookie texture.
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda: Gives the cookies a little rise, making them light and fluffy.
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar: Adds the tangy flavor that makes snickerdoodles so special.
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt: Just a pinch to bring out all the other flavors.
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil (melted): A vegan-friendly fat that keeps the cookies soft and rich.
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar: A natural sweetener with a hint of caramel flavor.
  • 1/4 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk): Adds moisture to the dough, keeping it soft and pliable.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract: The secret to adding that comforting, warm flavor to your cookies.
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed + 6 tablespoons water (flax egg): A perfect vegan egg replacement that binds everything together.
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon (for rolling): Adds that irresistible snickerdoodle flavor!
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (for rolling): Adds a sweet, crunchy coating to each cookie.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Heat your oven to 350°F (175°C), so it’s ready to bake your cookies to perfection.
  2. Prepare the flax egg: In a small bowl, mix 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed with 6 tablespoons of water. Let it sit for 5 minutes to thicken and turn into a gel-like consistency.
  3. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, cream of tartar, and salt.
  4. Combine wet ingredients: In another bowl, mix the melted coconut oil, coconut sugar, almond milk, vanilla extract, and the flax egg. Stir until everything is smooth and combined.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients: Add the wet mixture to the dry ingredients and stir until the dough comes together. You may need to use your hands to mix it well.
  6. Chill the dough: Cover the dough and refrigerate for 15-20 minutes. This helps the dough hold its shape while baking.
  7. Prepare cinnamon-sugar coating: Mix the cinnamon and granulated sugar in a small bowl. This is what will make your cookies sparkle with sweetness!
  8. Roll the dough into balls: Scoop 1 tablespoon of dough and roll it into a ball. Coat the ball evenly in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  9. Place on the baking sheet: Arrange the dough balls on a parchment-lined baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  10. Bake: Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden and the centers are slightly soft.
  11. Cool: Let the cookies cool for 5 minutes on the baking sheet, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
