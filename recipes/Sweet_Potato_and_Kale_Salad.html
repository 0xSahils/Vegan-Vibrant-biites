Sweet Potato and Kale Salad

This Sweet Potato and Kale Salad is a vibrant celebration of wholesome ingredients and bold flavors. Tender, caramelized roasted sweet potatoes pair perfectly with crisp, nutrient-packed kale, creating a delightful mix of textures. The addition of crispy chickpeas brings a satisfying crunch, while the tangy dressing ties all the elements together beautifully. This salad is more than just a side dish—it’s a complete meal that’s as nourishing as it is delicious. Packed with vitamins, fiber, and plant-based protein, it’s perfect for those seeking a balanced and flavorful option for lunch or dinner. The earthy sweetness of the roasted sweet potatoes contrasts wonderfully with the slightly bitter kale and the spiced crunch of the chickpeas, offering a dynamic taste in every bite. Whether served as a light main dish or a hearty side, this salad is a versatile choice for any occasion. Its colorful presentation and robust flavors make it a crowd-pleaser, while the simple preparation ensures it’s a go-to recipe for busy days. Elevate it further by adding a sprinkle of nuts, seeds, or a dollop of vegan yogurt for a creamy finish. This Sweet Potato and Kale Salad is sure to become a favorite in your healthy recipe repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas (or 1 can, drained and rinsed)
  • 4 cups kale, washed and chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • Water, as needed to thin the dressing

Instructions

  1. Roast the Sweet Potatoes: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the cubed sweet potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread them on a baking sheet in a single layer. Roast for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and slightly caramelized, stirring halfway through.
  2. Prepare the Chickpeas: While the sweet potatoes are roasting, toss the chickpeas with a little olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread them on a separate baking sheet and roast for 15-20 minutes, or until crispy.
  3. Massage the Kale: In a large bowl, add the chopped kale and drizzle with a small amount of olive oil. Massage the kale for 2-3 minutes to soften it and make it more tender.
  4. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, and cumin. Add water a little at a time until the dressing reaches your desired consistency.
  5. Assemble the Salad: In a large bowl, combine the roasted sweet potatoes, roasted chickpeas, massaged kale, and sliced red onion. Drizzle the dressing over the top and toss everything together to combine.
  6. Top with Seeds: Sprinkle pumpkin seeds over the salad for an extra crunch, and serve immediately or store in the fridge for up to 3 days.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 12g
  • Sodium: 300mg
