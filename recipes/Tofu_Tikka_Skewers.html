Vegan Tofu Tikka Skewers

These Tofu Tikka Skewers are a delectable vegan take on the classic Indian Tikka, offering all the smoky, aromatic flavors you love with a plant-based twist. The tofu is marinated in a rich, fragrant blend of spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala, allowing it to absorb all the delicious flavors. After marinating, the tofu is grilled to perfection, creating a crispy, charred exterior while maintaining a tender, juicy interior. Perfect as a starter, snack, or a protein-packed main course, these skewers are bursting with bold flavors. Serve them with warm naan bread, fragrant basmati rice, or a side of cooling cucumber raita for a complete and satisfying meal. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or enjoying a cozy meal at home, these Tofu Tikka Skewers will surely impress everyone with their vibrant colors and delicious taste. It's also versatile enough to be served with a variety of sides or tossed into a salad for a lighter option.

Ingredients

  • 400g firm tofu (pressed and cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup vegan yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp chopped cilantro (for garnish)
  • Wooden or metal skewers (for grilling)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the tofu: Press the tofu to remove excess moisture. Cut the tofu into cubes and set aside.
  2. Make the marinade: In a bowl, combine the vegan yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and pepper. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Marinate the tofu: Add the tofu cubes to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight, for the best flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill: Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium heat. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 20 minutes before use.
  5. Assemble the skewers: Thread the marinated tofu onto the skewers, ensuring the pieces are tightly packed together.
  6. Grill the skewers: Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the tofu is golden and slightly charred.
  7. Serve: Garnish the skewers with chopped cilantro and serve with naan, rice, or a side salad.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 200
  • Protein: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 12g
  • Fat: 14g
  • Fiber: 4g
