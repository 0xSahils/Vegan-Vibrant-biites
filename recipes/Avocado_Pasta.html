Vegan Avocado Pasta

This Vegan Avocado Pasta is a creamy, plant-based pasta dish that brings together the richness of ripe avocados with the zesty freshness of garlic, lemon, and fresh herbs. The creamy avocado sauce is both nutritious and indulgent, offering a perfect balance of flavors without the need for any dairy or heavy cream. It’s an excellent choice for anyone looking to enjoy a quick, healthy, and satisfying meal. The base of the sauce is made from ripe avocados, which are blended until smooth and velvety, providing a rich, buttery texture that coats the pasta beautifully. The addition of fresh garlic and a squeeze of lemon juice gives the sauce a savory and slightly tangy kick that complements the creaminess of the avocado. Fresh herbs such as basil or parsley can be added for an extra burst of flavor and color. What makes this recipe so appealing is how customizable it is. You can choose your favorite type of pasta – from spaghetti to penne – or even opt for gluten-free varieties. You can also boost the nutritional value by adding sautéed vegetables like spinach, zucchini, or cherry tomatoes. This dish is not only satisfying but also packed with healthy fats, fiber, and essential vitamins. Whether you're looking for a light lunch, a quick dinner, or a dish to impress guests, Vegan Avocado Pasta is the perfect solution. Its creamy texture, vibrant color, and bold flavors make it a delightful option for any occasion.

Ingredients List

  • 12 oz pasta: Choose your favorite type of pasta, such as spaghetti, penne, or fusilli, for the perfect base.
  • 2 ripe avocados: These creamy avocados are the star of the dish – simply pit and peel them for a smooth texture.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced for that aromatic, savory kick that enhances the flavor of the sauce.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: Adds richness and helps bring all the ingredients together in the sauce.
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice: Freshly squeezed to bring a bright, tangy note that balances the creaminess of the avocado.
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil: Chopped basil adds a burst of color and a fragrant herbal flavor to the dish.
  • Salt and pepper: Season to taste – these essentials elevate all the flavors in the recipe.
  • Red pepper flakes: Optional, but they can add a gentle heat to enhance the overall flavor profile.
  • Vegan Parmesan: For a final touch, sprinkle vegan Parmesan on top for a cheesy, nutty flavor, if desired.

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta: In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions. Drain, reserving some pasta water for the sauce.
  2. Prepare the avocado sauce: In a blender or food processor, combine the avocado, garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, and basil. Blend until smooth. If the sauce is too thick, add a bit of pasta water to reach your desired consistency.
  3. Mix the pasta and sauce: Toss the cooked pasta with the avocado sauce until well-coated. If needed, add a bit of reserved pasta water to help the sauce adhere to the pasta.
  4. Season: Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to taste.
  5. Serve: Serve the pasta in bowls, topped with additional fresh basil and vegan Parmesan if desired.
