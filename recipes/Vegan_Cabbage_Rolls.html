Vegan Cabbage Rolls

Enjoy these hearty and flavorful Vegan Cabbage Rolls, packed with a savory filling of rice, lentils, and vegetables, all wrapped in tender cabbage leaves. These plant-based rolls are a comforting and satisfying dish that’s perfect for a cozy dinner or a special occasion. The filling is seasoned with aromatic herbs and spices like garlic, onion, and paprika, adding depth and richness to every bite. Once rolled up, the cabbage rolls are simmered in a delicious tomato-based sauce, which infuses them with even more flavor. Vegan Cabbage Rolls are not only delicious but also packed with plant-based protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, making them a healthy choice for any meal. You can easily customize the filling by adding your favorite veggies or swapping in quinoa or another grain for variety. Whether served on their own or alongside a fresh salad or crusty bread, Vegan Cabbage Rolls are a satisfying, wholesome, and filling dish that everyone will love. Perfect for meal prep or as a main course for a family dinner, this recipe is sure to become a new favorite in your plant-based repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cabbage: Outer leaves peeled for wrapping.
  • 1 cup cooked rice: Use your favorite variety.
  • 1 can black beans: Drained and rinsed.
  • 1 onion: Chopped finely.
  • 2 garlic cloves: Minced.
  • 1 tsp cumin: Adds a smoky flavor.
  • 1 tsp paprika: For color and depth.
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce: For flavor and binding.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • Fresh herbs: Optional, for garnish.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the cabbage: Boil the cabbage leaves until soft and pliable.
  2. Make the filling: In a bowl, combine rice, black beans, onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, and tomato sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Assemble the rolls: Place a spoonful of the filling onto each cabbage leaf and roll tightly.
  4. Cook the rolls: Arrange the rolls in a baking dish and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs if desired and enjoy!
