Vegan Curry Noodles

Dive into a bowl of flavorful Vegan Curry Noodles, a vibrant and satisfying dish that brings together the bold flavors of curry with the comfort of noodles. This plant-based recipe features a creamy, spiced coconut curry sauce that coats every strand of noodle, creating a rich and aromatic meal. Packed with wholesome ingredients, these noodles include sautéed vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots for a burst of color and crunch. The sauce combines creamy coconut milk, red or yellow curry paste, and a hint of lime juice for a perfect balance of spice, tang, and creaminess. For added protein, toss in tofu, chickpeas, or tempeh, making it a hearty and well-rounded dish. Easy to customize, you can use your favorite noodles—rice noodles, soba, or even zucchini noodles—for a gluten-free option. Garnish with fresh cilantro, lime wedges, and a sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds for an extra layer of flavor. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or meal prep, Vegan Curry Noodles are a comforting, healthy, and versatile dish that will transport your taste buds straight to a world of bold and exotic flavors.

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles: Or your favorite noodles.
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil: For sautéing.
  • 1 onion: Chopped.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced.
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger: Grated.
  • 1 red bell pepper: Sliced.
  • 1 cup broccoli florets: Steamed.
  • 1 medium carrot: Julienned or sliced thinly.
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk: Full-fat for richness.
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce: For seasoning.
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder: Adds warmth and flavor.
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter: Optional, for extra creaminess.
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice: For a zesty kick.
  • Fresh cilantro: Chopped, for garnish.
  • Sesame seeds: For garnish.
  • Optional: Red chili flakes for a spicy touch.

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles: Follow the package instructions to cook the rice noodles. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil: In a large pan or wok, heat the sesame oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, and sauté for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the veggies: Add the red bell pepper, broccoli, and carrot to the pan. Stir-fry for 5 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, soy sauce, curry powder, and peanut butter (if using).
  5. Combine everything: Pour the sauce into the pan with the vegetables. Stir well to coat everything in the sauce. Let it simmer for 3-4 minutes to allow the flavors to combine.
  6. Toss the noodles: Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss them in the curry sauce until well combined. Let everything heat through for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Finish and serve: Stir in the lime juice, and garnish with fresh cilantro, sesame seeds, and red chili flakes if desired. Serve hot.
