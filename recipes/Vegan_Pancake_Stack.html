Vegan Pancake Stack

Start your day right with these fluffy and delicious Vegan Pancakes. Made with wholesome, plant-based ingredients, these pancakes are light, airy, and incredibly satisfying. The recipe uses simple ingredients like flour, plant-based milk, baking powder, and a touch of vanilla, making them perfect for anyone following a vegan lifestyle. You can easily customize your pancakes with different toppings—whether you like them drizzled with maple syrup, fresh berries, sliced bananas, or sprinkled with crunchy nuts like almonds or walnuts. These pancakes are not only a delicious breakfast treat but also a great way to bring the whole family together for a fun and cozy weekend brunch. Whether served with a side of fresh fruit or your favorite vegan butter, this vegan pancake recipe is a crowd-pleaser. They’re versatile, easy to make, and provide a great start to your day. Plus, they’re completely free from dairy, eggs, and other animal products, so they’re suitable for anyone looking for a cruelty-free breakfast option. Try them once, and you’ll be making them every weekend!

Ingredients

Here's what you'll need to whip up a stack of these irresistible vegan pancakes:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour: A versatile base for fluffy pancakes.
  • 1 tbsp baking powder: Adds lift and fluffiness.
  • 1/4 tsp salt: Enhances the flavor of the pancakes.
  • 1 tbsp sugar: Optional, for a touch of sweetness.
  • 1 cup plant-based milk: Almond, soy, or oat milk works great.
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract: Adds a hint of warmth and sweetness.
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil: Such as vegetable or coconut oil, for a soft texture.

Instructions

  1. Combine dry ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Mix wet ingredients: In another bowl, combine the plant-based milk, vanilla extract, and oil.
  3. Make the batter: Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Avoid overmixing.
  4. Heat the skillet: Lightly oil a non-stick skillet and heat over medium-low heat.
  5. Cook the pancakes: Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Stack and serve: Stack the pancakes on a plate and top with fresh fruits, nuts, and syrup. Serve warm and enjoy!

Nutrition Information (per pancake)

  • Calories: 110
  • Protein: 2g
  • Carbohydrates: 18g
  • Fat: 3g
  • Fiber: 1g
