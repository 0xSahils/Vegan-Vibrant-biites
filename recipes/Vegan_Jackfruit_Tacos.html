Vegan Jackfruit Tacos

These Vegan Jackfruit Tacos are the perfect combination of flavor and texture, offering a satisfying plant-based twist on traditional tacos. The jackfruit, when shredded, mimics the texture of pulled pork, making it an ideal meat substitute. Marinated in a blend of spices and tangy BBQ sauce, the jackfruit is cooked until tender and flavorful, creating a smoky and savory filling for your tacos. Served on soft tortillas, these tacos are topped with fresh ingredients like crunchy cabbage, creamy avocado, and zesty salsa, which add vibrant colors and complementary textures. A squeeze of lime brings a refreshing citrusy finish that elevates the flavors. You can easily customize the toppings to suit your preferences, whether you add pickled onions, cilantro, or a dollop of dairy-free sour cream. Vegan Jackfruit Tacos are not only a fun and delicious meal, but they are also packed with nutrients like fiber, antioxidants, and essential vitamins. This dish is perfect for Taco Tuesdays, casual dinners, or a fun meal with friends and family. Simple to prepare yet bursting with flavor, these tacos are sure to become a new favorite in your plant-based recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans young green jackfruit in brine (drained and shredded)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 cup fresh salsa
  • 1/2 cup red cabbage (shredded)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the jackfruit: Drain and rinse the jackfruit. Use your hands or a fork to shred it into pieces, removing any seeds.
  2. Cook the filling: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and saut√© for 2-3 minutes until soft. Add the shredded jackfruit, smoked paprika, cumin, chili powder, oregano, vegetable broth, and soy sauce. Stir to combine and cook for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the jackfruit to become tender. Stir occasionally and add more broth if needed.
  3. Prepare the tortillas: Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable.
  4. Assemble the tacos: Fill each tortilla with the jackfruit mixture. Top with fresh salsa, sliced avocado, shredded red cabbage, and chopped cilantro.
  5. Serve: Serve the tacos with a squeeze of lime and enjoy!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 6g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 8g
