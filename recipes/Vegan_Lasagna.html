Vegan Lasagna

This Vegan Lasagna is a comforting and hearty plant-based twist on the classic Italian dish. Layers of tender pasta sheets are stacked with rich tomato sauce, savory vegan ricotta made from tofu or cashews, and a flavorful mixture of sautéed vegetables such as mushrooms, spinach, and zucchini. The combination of flavors and textures creates a satisfying and nourishing meal that will delight both vegans and non-vegans alike. The marinara sauce, simmered with garlic, onions, and fresh herbs, is perfectly complemented by the creamy vegan ricotta, giving the lasagna a creamy, melt-in-your-mouth texture. Topped with a layer of vegan mozzarella cheese, the lasagna bakes to golden perfection, offering a deliciously crispy crust and a gooey, cheesy interior. This Vegan Lasagna is not only delicious but also customizable. You can add in your favorite vegetables or protein sources like lentils or tempeh to make it even heartier. It's the perfect dish for family gatherings, potlucks, or meal prep, as it can be made ahead of time and stored in the fridge for easy reheating. Indulge in this comforting, plant-based lasagna that will satisfy your cravings for rich, cheesy comfort food without any dairy or meat. It’s a perfect, wholesome meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups cashew cheese
  • 1 cup sautéed spinach
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup vegan mozzarella cheese (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the filling: Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the mushrooms and spinach until tender. Season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, followed by cashew cheese, sautéed vegetables, and more sauce. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce and vegan mozzarella.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes to brown the top.
  6. Cool and serve: Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 420
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Sodium: 780mg
