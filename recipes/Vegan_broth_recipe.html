Vegan Broth

This vegan broth is a nourishing and versatile base for soups, stews, and other dishes. Packed with fresh vegetables, herbs, and spices, it's a must-have for any plant-based kitchen. Use it as a standalone beverage or enhance your favorite recipes with its rich, savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 large carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 8 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, thyme) for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash and chop all the vegetables into large chunks for easy handling.
  2. Simmer the broth: In a large pot, combine all ingredients except salt and fresh herbs. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and let it simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Strain the broth: Remove the pot from heat and strain the liquid through a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth. Discard the solids.
  4. Season and serve: Add salt to taste and garnish with fresh herbs if desired. Use immediately or store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days or freeze for longer storage.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 40
  • Protein: 2g
  • Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Fat: 0g
  • Fiber: 2g
  • Sodium: 120mg
