Avocado Cucumber Salad

This Avocado Cucumber Salad is a harmonious blend of freshness and flavor, designed to delight your taste buds while nourishing your body. Perfect for any occasion, this salad works wonderfully as a light summer meal, a refreshing side dish, or even a snack that feels indulgent yet wholesome. The star of this recipe is the creamy avocado, which provides a satisfying richness and a dose of heart-healthy fats. Combined with the crisp, hydrating cucumber, this pairing creates a texture contrast that is both pleasing and satisfying. The addition of thinly sliced red onion introduces a subtle sharpness, balancing the creamy and crisp elements. Fresh cilantro not only adds a vibrant burst of green but also imparts its unique citrusy notes that elevate the dish. What truly ties these ingredients together is the simple yet elegant lemon vinaigrette. Made with extra-virgin olive oil and freshly squeezed lemon juice, this dressing offers a bright and zesty finish, ensuring every bite feels refreshing and flavorful. This salad is more than just a recipe—it’s a celebration of simplicity and health. Whether you’re serving it at a casual lunch, packing it for a picnic, or pairing it with a hearty main course, this dish promises to impress. Easy to prepare and endlessly satisfying, it’s a testament to the beauty of fresh ingredients.

Ingredients List

  • 2 perfectly ripe avocados: Select avocados that are slightly soft when pressed to ensure they are creamy and ready to eat. Their smooth texture adds richness, while their healthy fats keep you satisfied.
  • 1 fresh cucumber: Use an English cucumber for a crisp, refreshing bite. Its subtle flavor complements the creaminess of the avocado without overpowering it. Slice it thinly for a polished, restaurant-style presentation.
  • 1/4 red onion: A touch of thinly sliced red onion adds a subtle sharpness and vibrant color, creating visual appeal and a slight zing in every bite.
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro:
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil:
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice:
  • Salt and freshly cracked black pepper:
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional):

Instruction to make

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Begin by gently peeling and dicing two ripe avocados into bite-sized chunks. For the cucumber, use a mandoline or a sharp knife to create thin, even slices. Slice a small red onion into delicate rings to add a subtle sharpness. Place all prepared vegetables in a large, elegant mixing bowl to keep them fresh and ready.
  2. Whisk the dressing: In a small, decorative bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil, the juice of one fresh lemon, a pinch of salt, a dash of freshly ground black pepper, and a sprinkle of chili flakes for a hint of heat. Use a small whisk or fork to blend the ingredients until the dressing is silky and smooth.
  3. Toss the salad: Drizzle the luscious dressing over the bowl of vegetables. Add a generous handful of finely chopped fresh cilantro for an aromatic touch. Using a large spoon or your hands, toss the salad gently to ensure the dressing coats every piece evenly without mashing the delicate avocado.
  4. Present and serve: For the best flavor, serve the salad immediately on a chilled platter or in individual bowls. Garnish with an extra sprinkle of chili flakes or a sprig of fresh cilantro for a pop of color. If preparing in advance, refrigerate for no more than 2 hours to preserve the salad’s fresh, vibrant taste. Enjoy the medley of textures and flavors!
