Vegan Falafel Wrap

Experience the vibrant flavors of the Middle East with this irresistible Vegan Falafel Wrap. Each wrap features crispy, golden-brown falafels made from a blend of chickpeas, fresh herbs, and aromatic spices, delivering a perfect balance of texture and taste. Wrapped in a warm pita or soft tortilla, this wholesome dish is layered with an assortment of fresh vegetables like crunchy lettuce, juicy tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, and tangy pickled onions. To elevate the flavor profile, the wrap is generously drizzled with creamy tahini sauce or a rich hummus spread, adding a smooth and nutty contrast to the spiced falafel. For an extra kick, try topping it with zesty garlic sauce, a dash of hot sauce, or even a sprinkle of sumac. This wrap is not only packed with flavor but also offers a balanced and nutritious meal, providing plant-based protein, fiber, and essential vitamins. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or even a hearty snack, it’s a versatile option that can be customized with your favorite toppings like olives, roasted peppers, or a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Whether you’re on-the-go or enjoying a meal at home, the Vegan Falafel Wrap is a satisfying and delicious choice for anyone craving a plant-based twist on a classic dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying
  • 4 whole wheat wraps
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup tahini sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the falafel mixture: Drain the soaked chickpeas and add them to a food processor along with parsley, garlic, onion, flour, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper. Pulse until the mixture is combined but not pureed.
  2. Form the falafel balls: Shape the mixture into small balls or patties.
  3. Cook the falafel: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the falafel until golden brown and crispy on all sides, about 3-4 minutes per side. Drain on a paper towel.
  4. Assemble the wrap: Lay out a whole wheat wrap. Add lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, and falafel. Drizzle with tahini sauce.
  5. Roll and serve: Roll the wrap tightly, slice in half, and serve. Enjoy your Vegan Falafel Wrap!

Nutrition Information (per wrap)

  • Calories: 360
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 42g
  • Fat: 14g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Sodium: 320mg
