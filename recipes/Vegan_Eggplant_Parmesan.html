Vegan Eggplant Parmesan

Indulge in a hearty and flavorful Vegan Eggplant Parmesan, a plant-based twist on the classic Italian dish. This comforting meal features crispy, golden-brown eggplant slices that are coated in a seasoned breadcrumb mixture and baked to perfection. Layered with rich marinara sauce and melted vegan cheese, it delivers all the savory goodness of traditional Eggplant Parmesan without any dairy or meat. The eggplant itself becomes tender and flavorful while maintaining its satisfying texture, and the marinara sauce adds a tangy, herbaceous touch to each bite. Whether you’re serving it over a bed of pasta, enjoying it as a main course with a side salad, or simply savoring it on its own, this vegan version of Eggplant Parmesan will quickly become a favorite in your recipe collection. Perfect for dinner parties, weeknight meals, or special occasions, this dish is a crowd-pleaser that showcases how delicious plant-based eating can be. It’s rich in flavor, satisfying, and incredibly easy to make, with layers of wholesome ingredients that will leave you coming back for more. A true comfort food that proves you don’t have to sacrifice flavor for a vegan lifestyle!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium eggplants: Slice into 1/2-inch thick rounds.
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs: Use gluten-free breadcrumbs for a gluten-free version.
  • 1/2 cup vegan Parmesan cheese: For sprinkling over the eggplant layers.
  • 1 1/2 cups marinara sauce: Use a store-bought or homemade marinara sauce.
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese: Shredded, for a cheesy topping.
  • 1/2 cup flour: For dredging the eggplant slices.
  • 1 cup plant-based milk: For dipping the eggplant slices.
  • Olive oil: For frying the eggplant slices.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste, for seasoning.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the eggplant: Slice the eggplants into rounds and sprinkle with salt. Let them sit for 10-15 minutes to release moisture, then pat them dry.
  2. Set up dredging station: In one bowl, place the flour. In another, pour the plant-based milk. In a third bowl, combine breadcrumbs and vegan Parmesan cheese.
  3. Coat the eggplant: Dip each eggplant slice into flour, then the milk, and finally the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
  4. Fry the eggplant: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the eggplant slices for 2-3 minutes per side, until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Assemble the casserole: In a baking dish, layer fried eggplant slices, marinara sauce, and vegan mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  6. Bake: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake for 20-25 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve: Remove from the oven, let cool slightly, and serve hot. Garnish with extra vegan Parmesan if desired.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 5g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 7g
