Coconut Lime Rice

This Coconut Lime Rice is a fragrant and flavorful dish that blends the creamy richness of coconut milk with the bright zestiness of lime. Perfectly fluffy and infused with tropical vibes, it’s a versatile side dish that pairs beautifully with spicy curries, grilled vegetables, or tangy tacos. Whether you’re preparing a quick weekday meal or a festive dinner, this rice is an easy way to elevate your table. Enjoy it as a comforting base for your favorite dishes or as a refreshing stand-alone side.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice: Provides a fragrant, long-grain base for the rice.
  • 1 cup coconut milk: Adds a creamy texture and a rich coconut flavor.
  • 1/2 cup water: Used to cook the rice and balance the coconut milk.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: To sauté the rice before cooking.
  • 1/2 tsp salt: To season the rice.
  • 1/2 tsp lime zest: Adds a fresh, zesty lime flavor.
  • 2 tbsp fresh lime juice: Provides acidity and brightness.
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro: For garnish and a fresh, herbal note.

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice: Place the basmati rice in a fine-mesh sieve and rinse under cold water until the water runs clear. This helps remove excess starch and ensures fluffy rice.
  2. Cook the rice: In a medium saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the rinsed rice and sauté for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently, to lightly toast the grains and enhance their flavor.
  3. Add liquids: Carefully pour in the coconut milk, water, and a pinch of salt. Stir to combine and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Simmer: Reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan with a lid, and let the rice cook for 15-20 minutes. Check occasionally to ensure the liquid is absorbed, and the rice is tender.
  5. Finish with lime: Remove the saucepan from heat and let it sit covered for 5 minutes. Fluff the rice gently with a fork, then stir in lime zest, lime juice, and freshly chopped cilantro for a burst of flavor.
