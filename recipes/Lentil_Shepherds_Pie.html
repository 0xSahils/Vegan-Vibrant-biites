Vegan Lentil Shepherd's Pie

This Vegan Lentil Shepherd's Pie is the ultimate comfort food, featuring a rich and hearty lentil filling topped with a smooth and creamy mashed potato topping. It's a delicious, plant-based twist on the classic shepherd's pie, made with wholesome ingredients like lentils, carrots, peas, and onions. It's a great dish for a cozy family dinner or meal prep, and it's easy to make ahead and reheat. Vegan, gluten-free, and full of flavor, this dish will satisfy your taste buds while being healthy and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils (rinsed)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce or tamari (for gluten-free)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 large potatoes, peeled and cubed (for the mashed potatoes)
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter or olive oil (for the mashed potatoes)
  • Salt to taste (for mashed potatoes)
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the lentils: In a medium saucepan, combine the lentils and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cover. Cook for 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender and most of the liquid is absorbed. Drain any excess liquid if needed and set the lentils aside.
  2. Prepare the mashed potatoes: In a large pot, bring the peeled and cubed potatoes to a boil in salted water. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are fork-tender. Drain and return the potatoes to the pot. Add the almond milk, vegan butter, and salt. Mash until smooth and creamy. Set aside.
  3. Cook the vegetables: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and carrots, and cook for 5-7 minutes, until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Add the lentils and seasonings: Stir in the cooked lentils, tomato paste, soy sauce, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Add the peas and cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  5. Assemble the shepherd's pie: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread the lentil and vegetable mixture evenly in the bottom of a baking dish. Top with the mashed potatoes, spreading them evenly over the lentil filling. Use a fork to create a textured surface on top of the potatoes.
  6. Bake: Bake the shepherd's pie in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and slightly crispy. If desired, broil the top for 2-3 minutes for a crispier finish.
  7. Serve: Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy your hearty and comforting vegan lentil shepherd's pie!

  • Protein: 14g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Fiber: 10g
