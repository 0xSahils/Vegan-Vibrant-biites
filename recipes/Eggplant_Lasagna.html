Vegan Eggplant Lasagna

This Vegan Eggplant Lasagna is a wholesome and delicious plant-based alternative to the traditional lasagna. Instead of the usual pasta sheets, we use thinly sliced, tender eggplant to create layers that are just as satisfying but much lighter. The eggplant is carefully roasted to bring out its natural sweetness and flavor, offering a hearty yet healthy base for the dish. Between these layers of eggplant, you’ll find a creamy, flavorful vegan ricotta made from a blend of cashews, tofu, and nutritional yeast, giving you that classic lasagna creaminess without any dairy. The marinara sauce is a rich blend of tomatoes, garlic, onions, and fresh basil, slow-simmered to develop a deep, savory flavor that ties everything together beautifully. The combination of the eggplant, vegan ricotta, and marinara sauce creates the perfect balance of textures and flavors in every bite. This lasagna is not only a great way to enjoy vegetables but also a fantastic way to introduce plant-based eating into your meals without sacrificing taste or comfort. It’s the perfect dish for family gatherings, dinner parties, or cozy nights in. Plus, it’s gluten-free, making it suitable for those with dietary restrictions. This Vegan Eggplant Lasagna will definitely become a staple in your plant-based recipe repertoire!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants (sliced lengthwise into 1/4 inch slices)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup vegan ricotta cheese (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the eggplant: Brush the eggplant slices with olive oil and place them on a baking sheet. Roast the eggplant in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and tender.
  3. Prepare the sauce: In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened. Add the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, oregano, salt, pepper, and tomato paste. Stir well and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer the ingredients starting with a layer of marinara sauce. Follow with a layer of roasted eggplant, a layer of vegan ricotta cheese, and a sprinkle of vegan mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Bake: Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil and bake for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes to brown the top. Let the lasagna cool for 5 minutes before serving.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 220
  • Protein: 7g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Sodium: 500mg – A moderate amount, depending on the marinara sauce used. Lower-sodium marinara can be substituted for a healthier alternative if desired.
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Rich in Vitamin A (from the marinara sauce) and Vitamin C (from the eggplant and tomatoes), as well as iron and calcium (from the vegan ricotta). These micronutrients are vital for immune function, skin health, and bone health.
