Vegan Stuffed Acorn Squash

This Vegan Stuffed Acorn Squash is a beautiful, hearty dish that combines roasted acorn squash with a flavorful quinoa stuffing. Sweet cranberries, crunchy nuts, and a touch of maple syrup make it the perfect combination of savory and sweet. Ideal for a cozy dinner or a festive gathering!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium acorn squashes: The star of the dish, tender and naturally sweet when roasted.
  • 1 cup quinoa (rinsed): A protein-packed, fluffy grain that serves as the base of the stuffing.
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries: Adds a sweet, tart pop of flavor to balance the savory squash.
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans or walnuts: Nuts bring a crunchy texture and earthy flavor.
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil: To roast the squash and give it a golden, crispy finish.
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup: A touch of sweetness to complement the natural flavors of the squash.
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon: Adds a warm, spicy note that pairs perfectly with squash.
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg: A hint of nutmeg for a cozy, festive flavor.
  • Salt and pepper to taste: For enhancing all the flavors and balancing the sweetness.
  • Fresh parsley (optional): A sprinkle of fresh herbs for a pop of color and freshness.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 400°F (200°C) to get it nice and hot for roasting the squash.
  2. Prepare the squashes: Slice each acorn squash in half, remove the seeds, and place them on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and pepper, and roast for 30-40 minutes until tender and caramelized.
  3. Cook the quinoa: While the squash is roasting, cook quinoa according to package instructions, usually 1 cup quinoa to 2 cups water. Once cooked, fluff with a fork.
  4. Prepare the stuffing: In a bowl, combine cooked quinoa, dried cranberries, chopped nuts, and maple syrup. Stir well to combine.
  5. Stuff the squashes: Once the squash halves are roasted and tender, remove them from the oven and fill the center with the quinoa stuffing. Press down gently to pack the stuffing in.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired, and enjoy this delicious, hearty dish!
