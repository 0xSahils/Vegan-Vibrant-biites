Jackfruit Pulled Pork Sandwiches

Dive into the rich and smoky flavors of Jackfruit Pulled Pork Sandwiches, a plant-based rendition of a classic barbecue favorite. Young jackfruit, with its naturally fibrous texture, is the star of this dish, perfectly mimicking the tenderness of pulled pork. Slowly simmered in a tangy, sweet, and slightly smoky BBQ sauce, the jackfruit absorbs every ounce of flavor, creating a deliciously satisfying filling. What sets this sandwich apart is the balance of textures and flavors. The smoky BBQ jackfruit is piled high on a toasted, fluffy bun and complemented by a layer of crunchy coleslaw. The creamy, tangy coleslaw not only adds a refreshing contrast but also enhances the smoky sweetness of the jackfruit, making each bite an explosion of flavor. Ideal for a variety of occasions, these sandwiches are perfect for backyard barbecues, family gatherings, or even a quick weeknight dinner. They’re easy to prepare, require minimal ingredients, and are sure to please a crowd, regardless of dietary preferences. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply exploring plant-based options, Jackfruit Pulled Pork Sandwiches offer a hearty and flavorful experience that doesn’t compromise on taste or texture. A must-try for every barbecue lover looking for a wholesome, vegan twist!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans young jackfruit: Drained and rinsed.
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce: Use your favorite vegan BBQ sauce.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing.
  • 1 onion: Finely chopped.
  • 2 garlic cloves: Minced.
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika: For added smokiness.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • 4 sandwich buns: Toasted.
  • Coleslaw: For topping (optional).

Instructions

  1. Prepare the jackfruit: Drain and rinse the canned jackfruit, then pat dry. Remove any hard seeds and shred the jackfruit using your hands or a fork to achieve a pulled pork-like texture.
  2. Sauté the aromatics: In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion and minced garlic, cooking until they become fragrant and translucent, about 3-5 minutes.
  3. Cook the jackfruit: Add the shredded jackfruit to the skillet along with 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika, 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin, salt, and black pepper to taste. Stir everything together and cook for 5 minutes, allowing the spices to infuse into the jackfruit.
  4. Add BBQ sauce: Pour 1 cup of your favorite vegan BBQ sauce over the jackfruit mixture, ensuring all pieces are evenly coated. Lower the heat and let the mixture simmer for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally. This allows the jackfruit to absorb the smoky and tangy flavors of the sauce.
  5. Toast the buns: While the jackfruit simmers, lightly toast the sandwich buns in a toaster or on a hot skillet until golden and crisp.
  6. Assemble the sandwiches: Spread a layer of vegan mayo or additional BBQ sauce on the bottom bun, then pile the BBQ jackfruit generously on top. Add a heaping spoonful of coleslaw for crunch and freshness, and finish with the top bun.
  7. Serve: Plate the sandwiches and serve immediately, pairing them with fries, a side salad, or pickles for a complete meal.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 6g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 8g
  • Fiber: 5g
