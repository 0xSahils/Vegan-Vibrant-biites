Quinoa Kale Salad

This Quinoa Kale Salad is a delightful and nutrient-packed dish that combines the best of healthy eating with bold, fresh flavors. The base of this salad is made from fluffy, protein-rich quinoa, which adds a satisfying texture and wholesome goodness to every bite. Paired with nutrient-dense kale, this salad ensures you’re getting a hearty dose of vitamins, minerals, and fiber, making it a fantastic choice for anyone looking to fuel their body with nourishing ingredients. What sets this salad apart is its tangy lemon vinaigrette, made with freshly squeezed lemon juice, olive oil, and a hint of garlic. The dressing not only enhances the natural flavors of the ingredients but also provides a burst of brightness that ties everything together beautifully. To add even more layers of flavor and texture, this salad features a medley of colorful vegetables, crunchy nuts or seeds, and dried fruits, making it as visually appealing as it is delicious. Whether you’re enjoying it as a standalone meal or serving it as a side dish, this Quinoa Kale Salad is perfect for any occasion. It’s quick to prepare, easy to customize with your favorite toppings, and versatile enough to fit into any diet. Packed with taste and nutrition, this salad is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed: A protein-packed base for the salad.
  • 4 cups kale, chopped: Fresh kale adds texture and nutrition.
  • 1 medium cucumber, diced: Adds crunch and freshness to the salad.
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved: For a burst of sweetness and color.
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced: Adds sharpness to balance the flavors.
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds: For crunch and extra nutrients.
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries (optional): Adds a sweet contrast to the salad.
  • 1/4 cup olive oil: For the vinaigrette dressing base.
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice: Adds tang and brightness to the dressing.
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar: For an extra zing in the dressing.
  • 1 tsp maple syrup: To sweeten the vinaigrette slightly.
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder: For flavor in the dressing.
  • Salt and pepper, to taste: For seasoning the salad and vinaigrette.

Instructions

  1. Cook the quinoa: In a medium saucepan, combine the quinoa and 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to a simmer. Cook for 15 minutes, or until the water is absorbed and the quinoa is tender. Fluff with a fork and let it cool.
  2. Prepare the salad ingredients: While the quinoa is cooking, chop the kale, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and red onion. Set aside.
  3. Make the vinaigrette: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss the salad: In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, chopped kale, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, sunflower seeds, and cranberries. Pour the vinaigrette over the salad and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately or refrigerate for later. This salad can be stored in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
