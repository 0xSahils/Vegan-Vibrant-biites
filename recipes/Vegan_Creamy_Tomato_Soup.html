Vegan Creamy Tomato Soup

Savor the comforting flavors of this Vegan Creamy Tomato Soup, a dairy-free twist on the classic favorite. This soup is made with ripe tomatoes, creamy coconut milk or cashew cream, and a blend of aromatic herbs and spices for a velvety, rich, and deeply satisfying experience. Perfectly balanced with a hint of sweetness and a touch of tang, this soup pairs beautifully with crusty bread or a vegan grilled cheese sandwich. Roasting the tomatoes enhances their natural sweetness, while sautéed onions and garlic create a flavorful base. The addition of basil and oregano gives this soup a classic Italian-inspired touch. Whether enjoyed as a cozy meal on a chilly day or as an appetizer for a dinner party, this Vegan Creamy Tomato Soup is easy to make and packed with wholesome ingredients. It’s gluten-free, nourishing, and can be customized with your favorite toppings like croutons, fresh basil, or a drizzle of olive oil. A timeless dish reimagined for everyone to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes, chopped: Fresh tomatoes provide the base for the soup, giving it a vibrant, tangy flavor.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: Used to sauté the onions and garlic, adding richness to the soup.
  • 1 medium onion, chopped: Onion adds depth and sweetness to the soup.
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced: Garlic infuses the soup with its savory and aromatic flavor.
  • 1 cup coconut milk: Adds creaminess and a subtle sweetness, balancing the acidity of the tomatoes.
  • 2 cups vegetable broth: The base liquid of the soup, adding savory depth to the flavor.(Learn how to make vegan broth here).
  • 1 tsp dried basil: A classic herb for tomato soup, adding a slightly sweet, savory note.
  • 1 tsp dried oregano: Provides an earthy, savory flavor that complements the tomatoes.
  • 1/2 tsp salt: Enhances the natural flavors of the ingredients.
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper: Adds a subtle heat to the soup.
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional): Balances the acidity of the tomatoes, enhancing the sweetness of the soup.
  • Fresh basil leaves, and roasted tofu for garnish (optional): Fresh basil adds a bright, herbaceous note as a garnish.

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté for about 5 minutes, until soft and translucent.
  2. Add the garlic: Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Cook the tomatoes: Add the chopped tomatoes to the pot and cook for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes break down and soften.
  4. Add the liquids and seasonings: Stir in the coconut milk, vegetable broth, dried basil, oregano, salt, black pepper, and sugar (if using). Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Blend the soup: Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth and creamy. Alternatively, transfer the soup to a blender in batches and blend until smooth.
  6. Taste and adjust: Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with more salt, pepper, or sugar if needed.
  7. Serve: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired. Serve hot with a slice of crusty bread or a side of salad.
