Sweet Potato Chili

This Sweet Potato Chili is the ultimate comfort food for chilly evenings, combining hearty ingredients with bold, warming spices to create a truly satisfying dish. The natural sweetness of tender sweet potatoes balances beautifully with the robust flavors of tomatoes, beans, and aromatic spices, resulting in a chili that’s both wholesome and rich in taste. Packed with fiber, protein, and essential vitamins, this vegan chili is as nutritious as it is delicious. It’s perfect for meal prep, as the flavors only deepen over time, making leftovers even more enjoyable. The spice level is fully customizable, so you can make it as mild or fiery as you like. Serve it steaming hot and garnish with a dollop of vegan sour cream, fresh cilantro, sliced avocado, or a squeeze of lime for an extra burst of flavor. This Sweet Potato Chili is not only a comforting dinner option but also a versatile recipe that works well for gatherings, game nights, or a cozy solo meal. Easy to prepare and deeply satisfying, it’s sure to become a staple in your plant-based recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes: Peeled and diced into small cubes.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing the vegetables.
  • 1 onion: Diced, for added flavor.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced, to bring out the savory flavor.
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz): Adds a rich base to the chili.
  • 1 can black beans (15 oz): Drained and rinsed.
  • 1 can kidney beans (15 oz): Drained and rinsed.
  • 1 cup vegetable broth: (How to make vegan broth here).For the right consistency and flavor.
  • 1 tsp cumin: For a warm, earthy flavor.
  • 1 tsp chili powder: Adds heat and depth to the chili.
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika: For a smoky undertone.
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper: Optional, for extra heat.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels: Adds sweetness and texture.
  • 1 tbsp lime juice: For a refreshing zing.
  • Fresh cilantro: Chopped, for garnish.
  • Vegan sour cream: Optional, for topping.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sweet potatoes: Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the diced sweet potatoes and sauté for 5-7 minutes until slightly softened.
  2. Cook the vegetables: Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Add the spices: Stir in the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using). Cook for 1-2 minutes to bring out the flavors.
  4. Combine the ingredients: Add the diced tomatoes, black beans, kidney beans, corn, and vegetable broth. Stir everything together and bring to a simmer.
  5. Simmer: Reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let the chili simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender and the flavors have melded together.
  6. Finish the chili: Stir in the lime juice and season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve: Ladle the chili into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. You can also top with vegan sour cream if desired. Enjoy your comforting Sweet Potato Chili!
