Quinoa and Black Bean Salad

This Quinoa and Black Bean Salad is a refreshing, protein-packed dish that’s as nutritious as it is delicious. Fluffy quinoa forms the base, combined with hearty black beans, sweet corn, cherry tomatoes, and crisp bell peppers for a medley of textures and colors. A tangy lime-cilantro dressing ties the ingredients together, adding a zesty kick and fresh herbal notes. Packed with fiber, plant-based protein, and essential nutrients, this salad is perfect as a light lunch, a side dish, or a potluck favorite. Easy to prepare and naturally gluten-free, it’s a versatile recipe that can be customized with your favorite toppings, like avocado, sliced jalapeños, or a sprinkle of pumpkin seeds. Serve chilled or at room temperature for a vibrant and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa: Rinse thoroughly to remove any bitterness.
  • 1 1/2 cups water: For cooking the quinoa to perfection.
  • 1 can (400g) black beans: Rinsed and drained for a hearty addition.
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes: Halved for a burst of sweetness.
  • 1 cup corn kernels: Use fresh, canned, or frozen for a touch of natural sweetness.
  • 1 red bell pepper: Diced to add a crunchy, vibrant touch.
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro: Chopped for a fresh and aromatic finish.
  • 3 tbsp olive oil: For a smooth and flavorful dressing.
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice: Adds a tangy zest to the dish.
  • 1 tsp ground cumin: Brings warm, earthy flavors.
  • Salt and black pepper: To taste, for seasoning perfection.

Instructions

  1. Cook the Quinoa: In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add the rinsed quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables: Dice the red bell pepper, halve the cherry tomatoes, and chop the cilantro.
  3. Combine Ingredients: In a large bowl, mix the cooked quinoa, black beans, cherry tomatoes, corn, bell pepper, and cilantro.
  4. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, cumin, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Toss the Salad: Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture. Toss gently until evenly coated.
  6. Serve: Enjoy immediately or refrigerate for an hour to let the flavors meld together.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 42g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Vitamin C: 60% of Daily Value
  • Iron: 15% of Daily Value
