Vegan Spaghetti Squash Stir Fry

This Vegan Spaghetti Squash Stir Fry is a delicious and healthy dish that brings together the mild, slightly sweet flavor of roasted spaghetti squash with a savory stir-fry sauce and a medley of fresh, crisp vegetables. It offers a light yet satisfying alternative to traditional stir fry, making it a great option for anyone looking to reduce their carb intake or follow a gluten-free diet. The spaghetti squash serves as the perfect base for the stir fry, providing a tender, noodle-like texture that absorbs the flavors of the sauce and pairs wonderfully with a variety of vegetables such as bell peppers, carrots, and broccoli. The stir fry sauce, made with a combination of soy sauce, sesame oil, and a touch of maple syrup, enhances the vegetables' natural sweetness while adding a rich umami depth. The dish is further elevated with a sprinkle of sesame seeds and fresh herbs for added flavor and texture. Whether you're following a plant-based diet or simply looking to enjoy a lighter, low-carb meal, this stir fry is the perfect balance of taste and nutrition. Packed with vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants from the veggies and squash, it's an excellent choice for a quick, easy, and nourishing meal. Plus, it's easily customizable with your favorite veggies or protein, making it versatile and great for meal prepping. Serve it as a main dish or as a side to your favorite Asian-inspired meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium spaghetti squash: Cooked and shredded into strands.
  • 2 tbsp olive oil: For stir-frying.
  • 1/2 onion: Thinly sliced.
  • 1 red bell pepper: Thinly sliced.
  • 1 cup snap peas: Trimmed.
  • 1 carrot: Julienned or thinly sliced.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced.
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce: Or tamari for gluten-free option.
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil: Adds depth of flavor.
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar: For a touch of acidity.
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup: Adds sweetness to balance flavors.
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes: For heat (optional).
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds: For garnish.
  • 1 tbsp fresh cilantro: Chopped, for garnish.
  • Fried Tofu cubes How to make roasted Tofu

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti squash: Cut the spaghetti squash in half, scoop out the seeds, and roast in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 30-40 minutes, or until tender. Once cooked, scrape out the flesh with a fork to create spaghetti-like strands. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the stir-fry: Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the onions, red bell pepper, snap peas, and carrots. Stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes, or until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  3. Add the garlic: Add minced garlic to the pan and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Make the sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, maple syrup, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  5. Combine the squash and sauce: Add the cooked spaghetti squash strands to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the sauce over the mixture and toss everything together to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the squash is heated through.
  6. Serve: Garnish with sesame seeds and fresh cilantro. Serve warm and enjoy!
