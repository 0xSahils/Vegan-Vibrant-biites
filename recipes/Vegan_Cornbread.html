Vegan Cornbread

Enjoy the comforting taste of this Vegan Cornbread, a golden, moist, and fluffy classic made entirely plant-based. Perfectly sweetened with a hint of natural sweetness and featuring a slightly crumbly texture, this cornbread pairs beautifully with hearty soups, stews, or a warm bowl of chili. Made with simple, wholesome ingredients like cornmeal, flour, plant-based milk, and a touch of maple syrup or sugar, this cornbread is easy to prepare and full of rich, buttery flavor without any dairy or eggs. You can customize it by adding diced jalapeños, fresh corn kernels, or a sprinkle of vegan cheese for a savory twist. Whether you’re serving it as a side dish at a holiday gathering or enjoying it as a snack with a dollop of vegan butter, this Vegan Cornbread is sure to be a hit. Quick, versatile, and absolutely delicious, it’s the perfect addition to your plant-based recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal: The base for the recipe.
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour: Adds structure.
  • 1/4 cup sugar: For a hint of sweetness.
  • 1 tbsp baking powder: Ensures fluffiness.
  • 1/2 tsp salt: Enhances flavor.
  • 1 cup almond milk: Or any plant-based milk.
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil: For moisture.
  • 2 tbsp ground flaxseed: Mixed with 6 tbsp water as an egg replacer.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Set to 375°F (190°C) and grease an 8x8-inch baking dish.
  2. Prepare flax eggs: Mix ground flaxseed with water in a small bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Mix wet ingredients: In another bowl, combine almond milk, vegetable oil, and flax eggs.
  5. Combine: Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients. Stir until just combined; avoid overmixing.
  6. Bake: Pour batter into prepared dish and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool: Let cornbread cool slightly before slicing and serving.
