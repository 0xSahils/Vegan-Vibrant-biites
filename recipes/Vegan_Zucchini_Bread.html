Vegan Zucchini Bread

This Vegan Zucchini Bread is moist, tender, and packed with flavor, making it the perfect treat for breakfast, a snack, or dessert. Made with wholesome plant-based ingredients, it’s a healthier take on the classic quick bread. The grated zucchini adds a natural moisture and a hint of sweetness, while the warm spices like cinnamon and nutmeg create a cozy and inviting flavor profile. Sweetened with natural ingredients like maple syrup or coconut sugar, this zucchini bread is a guilt-free indulgence. Whether enjoyed plain or with a dollop of vegan butter or nut butter, each slice is hearty, satisfying, and rich in nutrients. Perfectly versatile, you can add in extras like chopped nuts, raisins, or dairy-free chocolate chips for additional texture and flavor. Easy to make and freezer-friendly, this Vegan Zucchini Bread is a great way to use up summer’s zucchini bounty or to enjoy all year round. It pairs wonderfully with a cup of tea or coffee and is sure to become a family favorite. Whether shared with loved ones or enjoyed solo, this delicious zucchini bread is a plant-based delight everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups grated zucchini: Squeeze out excess moisture.
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour: Can use whole wheat flour for added nutrition.
  • 1/2 cup sugar: Use coconut sugar or brown sugar for a healthier option.
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup: Adds natural sweetness.
  • 1/2 cup plant-based milk: Almond or oat milk works well.
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil: Coconut oil or melted vegan butter are good substitutes.
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract: For a warm, sweet flavor.
  • 1 tsp baking powder and 1/2 tsp baking soda: For leavening.
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon: Optional, for a hint of spice.
  • Pinch of salt: Enhances overall flavor.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Set to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. Prepare zucchini: Grate zucchini and squeeze out excess moisture using a clean towel.
  3. Mix wet ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together sugar, maple syrup, plant-based milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Combine mixtures: Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture and mix until just combined. Fold in grated zucchini.
  6. Bake: Pour batter into the loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  7. Cool: Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
  8. Serve: Slice and enjoy plain or with your favorite vegan spread.
