Vegan Caesar Salad

Indulge in this creamy and tangy Vegan Caesar Salad, a plant-based twist on the classic favorite. Crisp romaine lettuce is tossed with crunchy, golden croutons and smothered in a rich, dairy-free Caesar dressing made with cashews, nutritional yeast, and a hint of garlic. The dressing delivers the perfect balance of creaminess and tang, providing that signature Caesar flavor without any animal products. Topped with a sprinkle of vegan parmesan and freshly cracked black pepper, this salad is the perfect meal for lunch or dinner. It’s light yet satisfying, full of flavor, and packed with nutrients—an ideal option for anyone looking for a healthier, vegan take on a beloved classic.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce: Wash, dry, and chop into bite-sized pieces.
  • 1/2 cup vegan mayonnaise: Use a high-quality plant-based mayo for the best flavor.
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice: Freshly squeezed for a bright, tangy flavor.
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast: Adds a cheesy, umami taste to the dressing.
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard: Provides a subtle spicy kick.
  • 1 tsp garlic powder: Enhances the savory depth of the dressing.
  • 1/4 cup olive oil: Extra virgin olive oil preferred for a rich, smooth consistency.
  • 1 cup croutons: Use store-bought or homemade, ensuring they’re vegan-friendly.
  • Salt and pepper to taste: Season the salad as desired.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dressing: In a medium-sized bowl, combine vegan mayonnaise, freshly squeezed lemon juice, nutritional yeast, Dijon mustard, and garlic powder. Whisk until the mixture is smooth and well blended.
  2. Add olive oil: Gradually whisk in the olive oil to achieve a creamy, emulsified dressing. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  3. Prepare the romaine lettuce: Wash the lettuce thoroughly, dry it using a salad spinner or kitchen towel, and chop into bite-sized pieces. Place in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Add croutons: Toss the chopped lettuce with vegan croutons. Ensure the croutons are evenly distributed throughout the salad.
  5. Dress the salad: Drizzle the prepared dressing over the lettuce and croutons. Use salad tongs or clean hands to gently mix, ensuring every piece is coated.
  6. Optional garnish: Sprinkle with vegan Parmesan cheese or additional nutritional yeast for extra flavor.
  7. Serve immediately: Transfer to serving plates or a large salad bowl. Enjoy your creamy, tangy Vegan Caesar Salad!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 190
  • Protein: 3g
  • Carbohydrates: 12g
  • Fat: 14g
  • Fiber: 2g
