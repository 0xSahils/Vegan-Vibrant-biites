Black Bean Burgers

These Black Bean Burgers are a wholesome, protein-packed vegan alternative to traditional meat burgers. They are the perfect choice for anyone looking for a satisfying, plant-based meal that doesn’t compromise on taste or texture. The base of these burgers is black beans, which are rich in fiber and protein, making them both filling and nutritious. The beans are seasoned with a blend of spices such as cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, and chili powder, giving the burgers a smoky, slightly spicy flavor that will leave you craving more. To bind everything together, we add a combination of breadcrumbs and a touch of olive oil, helping the patties hold their shape while crisping up nicely when cooked. The result is a crispy, golden exterior with a soft, flavorful interior that’s perfect for any burger lover. These black bean patties are versatile enough to be served in a classic burger bun or enjoyed as a patty in a salad or wrap. Top the burger with fresh ingredients like avocado , tomato, lettuce, pickles, or even a vegan cheese slice for extra flavor. You can also customize the seasoning and toppings to suit your taste. Whether you're having them for lunch, dinner, or as a meal prep for the week, these Black Bean Burgers are sure to become a favorite in your plant-based meal rotation. They are not only easy to make but also a crowd-pleasing dish that is both satisfying and nourishing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz) black beans: Drained and rinsed.
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs: To bind the mixture together.
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion: For flavor.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced.
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed: Mixed with 3 tbsp water to form a flax egg.
  • 1 tsp cumin: For seasoning.
  • 1/2 tsp paprika: For added smokiness.
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala: A blend of aromatic Indian spices for depth and richness.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • Olive oil: For cooking the burgers.
  • Burger buns: Vegan buns for serving.
  • Toppings: Fresh Cilantro Leaves, Lettuce, tomato, avocado, vegan mayo (optional).

Instructions

  1. Prepare the black beans: In a large bowl, mash the black beans with a fork or potato masher until mostly mashed but still a bit chunky.
  2. Make the flax egg: In a small bowl, mix the ground flaxseed with water and let it sit for 5 minutes to thicken.
  3. Mix the ingredients: Add the breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, pepper, garam masala and flax seeds to the mashed black beans. Stir everything together until well combined.
  4. Form the patties: Divide the mixture into 4-6 portions and shape each into a patty.
  5. Cook the patties: Heat a little olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Assemble the burgers: Place the cooked patties on vegan burger buns and top with your favorite toppings such as lettuce, tomato, avocado(sliced or mashed), and vegan mayo.
