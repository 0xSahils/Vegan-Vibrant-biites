Cashew Cream Pasta

This Cashew Cream Pasta is a luxurious and comforting dish that redefines plant-based dining. The rich, velvety sauce is crafted from blended cashews, which provide a creamy texture reminiscent of traditional dairy-based sauces. Infused with hints of garlic, nutritional yeast, and a touch of lemon juice, the sauce strikes the perfect balance of savory and tangy flavors. Whether paired with spaghetti, penne, or fettuccine, this pasta dish is a wholesome and satisfying meal that’s as nourishing as it is delicious. Ideal for weeknight dinners or special occasions, this recipe is a crowd-pleaser for vegans and non-vegans alike. Customize it further with your favorite add-ins, like sautéed mushrooms, roasted cherry tomatoes, or fresh spinach, to elevate the flavor profile. Packed with protein, healthy fats, and essential nutrients, this Cashew Cream Pasta offers indulgence without compromise. Serve it hot, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley or cracked black pepper, and enjoy a restaurant-quality meal in the comfort of your home.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice): The base for the pasta dish.
  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked for 2 hours: For a creamy, smooth sauce.
  • 1 cup water or vegetable broth: (How to make vegan broth here).To blend the cashews into a smooth cream.
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast: Adds a cheesy, savory flavor to the sauce.
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice: For a tangy kick in the sauce.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: Adds richness to the cream sauce.
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced: For flavor and depth in the sauce.
  • 1/2 tsp salt: To season the sauce.
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper: To add a bit of heat to the sauce.
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder: For added savory flavor in the sauce.
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika (optional): For a smoky undertone.
  • 1/3 cup of peas(optional): frozen peas and let them thaw at room temperature while the pasta cooks. For a bit more protein
  • Fresh basil (for garnish): To add a fresh touch and flavor.
  • Cherry tomatoes (for garnish): Adds color and a fresh burst of flavor.

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta: Boil water in a large pot and cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the cashew cream: In a blender, combine the soaked cashews, water or vegetable broth, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, onion powder, and smoked paprika. Blend until smooth and creamy. Adjust the consistency with more water if needed.
  3. Heat the sauce: Pour the cashew cream sauce into a pan and heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally with peas(optional)
  4. Combine the pasta and sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the pan with the heated cashew cream sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly in the sauce.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh basil, cherry tomatoes, and extra nutritional yeast if desired. Serve hot.
