Vegan Sushi

This Vegan Sushi is fresh, colorful, and bursting with flavor. Made with avocado, cucumber, and carrots, itâ€™s a healthy and satisfying meal that you can enjoy anytime.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice: Cooked and cooled.
  • 1 sheet nori: Seaweed for rolling the sushi.
  • 1 avocado: Sliced into strips.
  • 1 cucumber: Julienned.
  • 1 small carrot: Julienned.
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar: For seasoning the rice.
  • 1 tsp sugar: To sweeten the rice vinegar mixture.
  • 1/2 tsp salt: For the rice vinegar mixture.
  • Soy sauce: For dipping the sushi.
  • Pickled ginger: For serving.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sushi rice: Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Cook according to package instructions. Once cooked, mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, and stir into the rice. Let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Prepare the vegetables: Slice the avocado, cucumber, and carrot into thin strips.
  3. Assemble the sushi: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat with the shiny side facing down. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving about an inch at the top.
  4. Add the fillings: Lay a few strips of avocado, cucumber, and carrot across the center of the rice.
  5. Roll the sushi: Using the sushi mat, carefully roll the sushi from the bottom, applying gentle pressure. Seal the roll with a bit of water on the edge of the nori.
  6. Slice the sushi: Using a sharp knife, slice the roll into bite-sized pieces. Clean the knife after each cut to prevent sticking.
  7. Serve: Serve the sushi with soy sauce and pickled ginger on the side.
