Vegetable Lentil Soup

This hearty Vegetable Lentil Soup is the perfect plant-based comfort food, loaded with protein-packed lentils and a medley of vibrant vegetables. It’s a wholesome, satisfying dish that’s easy to prepare and bursting with flavor. The base of the soup features aromatic ingredients like garlic, onion, and celery sautéed in olive oil, which lay the foundation for its rich, savory flavor. A mix of carrots, tomatoes, and leafy greens brings a touch of natural sweetness and a pop of color, while earthy spices like cumin, smoked paprika, and thyme add depth to every spoonful. Lentils, the star of the dish, are a nutritional powerhouse, offering plenty of protein and fiber to keep you feeling full and nourished. Simmered in a flavorful vegetable broth, the lentils and veggies create a soup that’s both hearty and comforting. Perfect as a main course or a starter, this soup pairs beautifully with crusty bread or a side salad. It’s also meal-prep-friendly and freezer-safe, making it an ideal go-to for busy weeknights or a quick lunch. Warm, filling, and incredibly delicious, this Vegetable Lentil Soup is sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil: For sautéing the vegetables.
  • 1 onion: Chopped.
  • 2 carrots: Peeled and chopped.
  • 2 celery stalks: Chopped.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced.
  • 1 cup dry lentils: Rinsed and drained.
  • 1 zucchini: Chopped.
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes: With juice.
  • 1 teaspoon cumin: Ground, for earthy flavor.
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric: Adds warmth and color.
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika: For a smoky touch.
  • 4 cups vegetable broth: Or water for a lighter soup.(Learn how to make vegan broth here).
  • 1 cup spinach: Fresh or frozen.
  • 1 teaspoon salt: To taste.
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper: To taste.
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice: For a fresh finish.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic, sautéing for 5-7 minutes until softened.
  2. Add the lentils and spices: Stir in the dry lentils, cumin, turmeric, and smoked paprika, cooking for another 1-2 minutes to bloom the spices.
  3. Simmer the soup: Add the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and zucchini to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  4. Add the spinach: Stir in the spinach and cook for another 3-5 minutes until wilted.
  5. Season and serve: Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice to taste. Serve hot, with a drizzle of olive oil or a sprinkle of fresh herbs if desired.
