Vegan Pumpkin Pie

Indulge in the creamy, spiced perfection of Vegan Pumpkin Pie, a plant-based twist on the classic holiday dessert. This pie features a velvety filling made with pumpkin puree, warm spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger, and a hint of maple syrup for natural sweetness. All of this goodness is nestled in a flaky, golden vegan pie crust that’s just as delightful as the traditional version. Whether you're hosting a festive gathering or enjoying a quiet evening, this pie is sure to impress.

The filling is crafted without any dairy or eggs, using ingredients like coconut cream or cashews for a rich and creamy texture, while cornstarch or arrowroot powder helps achieve the perfect set. Not only is this pie delicious, but it’s also loaded with the nutritional benefits of pumpkin, including fiber and vitamin A. Serve it chilled or warm, topped with a dollop of vegan whipped cream or a sprinkle of powdered sugar for a finishing touch.

Ideal for Thanksgiving, Christmas, or any time you're craving a slice of autumn-inspired comfort, this Vegan Pumpkin Pie is the ultimate crowd-pleaser. One bite, and it’s bound to become a favorite tradition for years to come.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups canned pumpkin puree: The base of the filling, providing a smooth texture and rich flavor.
  • 1 cup full-fat coconut milk: Adds creaminess and richness to the filling.
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup: Natural sweetness to balance the spices in the pie filling.
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch: Acts as a thickening agent, helping the filling set properly.
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon: Provides a warm, spicy flavor that complements the pumpkin.
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger: Adds a bit of zing and depth to the pie filling.
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg: Enhances the warmth of the spices and gives the pie a cozy flavor.
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves: Adds a touch of earthiness to the spice blend.
  • 1/4 tsp salt: Enhances the flavors of the filling and balances the sweetness.
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract: Adds sweetness and a depth of flavor to the filling.
  • 1 prepared vegan pie crust: A store-bought or homemade crust that is dairy-free and vegan-friendly. Make sure it’s prebaked if required for your crust type.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the filling: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, coconut milk, maple syrup, cornstarch, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, salt, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth and well-combined.
  3. Fill the pie crust: Pour the pumpkin filling into the prepared vegan pie crust, spreading it out evenly.
  4. Bake the pie: Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the filling is set and the top is slightly firm to the touch. You can insert a toothpick into the center to check for doneness—it should come out clean.
  5. Cool the pie: Allow the pie to cool completely on a wire rack. It will continue to firm up as it cools.
  6. Chill the pie: After cooling to room temperature, place the pie in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the filling to set properly.
  7. Serve: Serve the pie chilled, topped with vegan whipped cream or dairy-free ice cream if desired. Enjoy your delicious, creamy vegan pumpkin pie!
