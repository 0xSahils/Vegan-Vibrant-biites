Vegan Alfredo Pasta

Indulge in this creamy and garlicky Vegan Alfredo Pasta, made with a luscious, velvety cashew-based sauce that delivers all the richness and comfort of traditional Alfredo, without any dairy. The creamy cashew sauce is infused with roasted garlic, nutritional yeast, and a touch of lemon for a balanced, savory flavor profile. Tossed with perfectly cooked pasta, this dish offers a satisfying, comforting meal that’s perfect for cozy nights or when you're craving something indulgent. Whether you're a long-time vegan or just looking to try something new, this Vegan Alfredo Pasta is a crowd-pleaser that proves you don’t need dairy to enjoy a rich, flavorful pasta dish. Top with fresh herbs, a sprinkle of vegan parmesan, or a side of roasted vegetables for an added burst of flavor and texture. This dish is not only plant-based but also gluten-free (when paired with gluten-free pasta), making it a versatile and nourishing choice for everyone at the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashews: Soaked in hot water for 20 minutes.
  • 3 garlic cloves: Minced.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing.
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk: For the sauce base.
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast: Adds cheesy flavor.
  • 1/2 tsp salt: To taste.
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper: For seasoning.
  • 12 oz pasta: Your choice of fettuccine or spaghetti.
  • Fresh parsley: Chopped, for garnish.

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Blend sauce: In a blender, combine soaked cashews, almond milk, nutritional yeast, salt, and black pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Sauté garlic: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add sauce: Pour the blended sauce into the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently, until heated through.
  5. Toss pasta: Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until evenly coated with the sauce.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm.
