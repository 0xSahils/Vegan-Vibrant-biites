Veggie Spring Rolls

These Veggie Spring Rolls are a fresh and vibrant dish, perfect for a light meal or appetizer. Packed with crisp vegetables, herbs, and a tangy dipping sauce, they’re a healthy and delicious option for any occasion. Each spring roll is a delightful combination of textures and flavors, featuring crunchy carrots, cool cucumber, fresh lettuce, and fragrant mint leaves, all wrapped in delicate rice paper. You can also add rice noodles or tofu for extra substance. Making these spring rolls is a fun and interactive process. Simply soak the rice paper in warm water until pliable, layer on the ingredients, and roll tightly into neat bundles. The result is a beautiful, translucent wrap showcasing the colorful fillings inside. The dipping sauce elevates the experience, offering a burst of complementary flavor. Whether it’s a classic peanut sauce, a tangy soy-ginger dip, or a spicy chili-lime blend, each bite becomes an explosion of taste. Serve these Veggie Spring Rolls as an appetizer, snack, or light meal. They’re great for parties, picnics, or a healthy weekday treat. Customize with your favorite veggies and sauces to create your perfect version of this refreshing dish.

Ingredients

  • 8-10 rice paper sheets: For wrapping the veggies.
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage: Adds crunch and texture.
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots: For a hint of sweetness and crunch.
  • 1/2 cucumber: Julienned, for freshness and crunch.
  • 1/2 avocado: Sliced, for creaminess.
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro: Chopped, for added flavor.
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint: Chopped, for a refreshing kick.
  • 1/4 cup rice vermicelli noodles: Cooked, for extra texture.
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce: For a touch of savory flavor.
  • 1 tsp sesame oil: For a nutty aroma and flavor.
  • 1 tbsp lime juice: For a tangy, zesty finish.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the rice paper: Fill a shallow dish with warm water. Submerge one rice paper sheet at a time in the water for about 10-15 seconds, or until it softens. Remove the sheet and place it on a clean surface.
  2. Assemble the spring rolls: Place a small handful of shredded cabbage, grated carrots, cucumber, avocado slices, cilantro, mint, and cooked rice vermicelli noodles in the center of the rice paper.
  3. Wrap the spring rolls: Fold in the sides of the rice paper, then roll it up tightly from the bottom, enclosing all the ingredients. Repeat this process for the remaining rice paper sheets and filling ingredients.
  4. Prepare the dipping sauce: In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, sesame oil, and lime juice to make a simple dipping sauce.
  5. Serve: Arrange the spring rolls on a plate and serve with the dipping sauce on the side. Enjoy!
