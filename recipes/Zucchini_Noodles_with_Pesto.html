Zucchini Noodles with Pesto

Zucchini Noodles with Pesto is a light, refreshing, and nutritious vegan dish thatâ€™s perfect for any meal. Made with spiralized zucchini noodles, this dish is tossed in a homemade vegan pesto sauce, making it both flavorful and satisfying. It's a great low-carb, gluten-free option for pasta lovers!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchinis, spiralized into noodles
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts (or walnuts, for a budget-friendly option)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast (for a cheesy flavor)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pesto sauce: In a food processor, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, nutritional yeast, and lemon juice. Pulse until the mixture is finely chopped. Gradually add the olive oil while processing until you get a smooth pesto sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Prepare the zucchini noodles: Using a spiralizer, spiralize the zucchinis into noodles. If you don't have a spiralizer, you can use a vegetable peeler to create long strips of zucchini.
  3. Toss the noodles with pesto: In a large bowl, toss the zucchini noodles with the pesto sauce until they are well coated.
  4. Garnish and serve: Garnish the dish with cherry tomatoes or any toppings of your choice. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh, light, and delicious meal!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 180
  • Protein: 5g
  • Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Sodium: 180mg
