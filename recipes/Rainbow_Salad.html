Rainbow Salad

The Rainbow Salad is a vibrant and nourishing dish that truly lives up to its name. Bursting with an array of colors from fresh vegetables, fruits, and crunchy toppings, this salad is as beautiful as it is delicious. Each ingredient has been thoughtfully chosen not only for its flavor but also for its nutritional benefits. Packed with essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, the Rainbow Salad is a powerhouse of health in every bite. The base is made with crisp greens and a medley of chopped vegetables such as bell peppers, cucumbers, and shredded carrots, offering a satisfying crunch. Juicy fruits like mango, strawberries, or pomegranate seeds add a touch of natural sweetness that perfectly complements the savory components. To finish, a variety of toppings such as roasted seeds, nuts, or crispy chickpeas provide texture and an extra dose of protein. This salad is tied together with a tangy dressing made from freshly squeezed citrus juice, a splash of olive oil, and a hint of maple syrup or mustard for depth. Whether you’re serving it as a starter, a side, or a light main course, the Rainbow Salad is an excellent choice for anyone looking for a healthy, refreshing, and visually stunning dish. Quick, versatile, and customizable, it’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed greens: A mix of spinach, arugula, and lettuce for a fresh base.
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes: Halved, for a burst of sweetness and color.
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots: Adds crunch and a vibrant orange color.
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper: Thinly sliced, for a sweet and crisp texture.
  • 1/4 cup yellow bell pepper: Adds a bright yellow color and sweetness.
  • 1/4 cup red cabbage: Shredded, for crunch and a beautiful purple hue.
  • 1/4 cup purple cabbage: Shredded, for extra crunch and color contrast.
  • 1/4 cup purple onion: Thinly sliced, for a zesty and sharp flavor.
  • 1/4 cup avocado: Sliced, for creaminess and healthy fats.
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels: Fresh or cooked, for sweetness and texture.
  • 1/4 cup roasted chickpeas: For added protein and crunch (optional).
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds: For a nutty flavor and crunch.
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro: Chopped, for a fresh and herbaceous flavor.
  • For the dressing:
  • 2 tbsp olive oil: For the base of the dressing.
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar: For tanginess.
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup: For a hint of sweetness.
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard: Adds a sharp flavor.
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder: For extra seasoning.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash and chop all the vegetables as described in the ingredients list.
  2. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Assemble the salad: In a large bowl, combine all the prepared vegetables, avocado, roasted chickpeas, and sesame seeds. Toss to mix evenly.
  4. Dress the salad: Drizzle the prepared dressing over the salad and toss again to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately. Enjoy your colorful and nutritious Rainbow Salad!
