Stuffed Mushroom Caps

These vegan stuffed mushroom caps are a delicious and savory dish that will surely impress your guests. They make the perfect appetizer or side dish for any occasion, from casual family dinners to special celebrations. The mushroom caps are stuffed with a flavorful mixture of breadcrumbs, garlic, fresh herbs, and nutritional yeast, creating a rich, cheesy-like flavor without any dairy. The combination of the earthy mushrooms and the hearty filling creates a satisfying bite that is both light and satisfying. With each bite, you'll experience a burst of umami flavors, making them irresistible to both vegans and non-vegans alike. Plus, they’re incredibly easy to prepare, requiring minimal ingredients and just a short amount of time in the oven. Whether you're looking for a quick snack or something to impress your guests at a party, these stuffed mushrooms are sure to become a favorite go-to recipe. Enjoy them on their own, or pair them with a fresh salad or dipping sauce for an extra layer of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 12 large mushrooms: Stems removed and reserved for the filling.
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs: For a crispy, golden stuffing.
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil: Adds richness and flavor.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced, for a burst of savory aroma.
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast: Adds a cheesy flavor.
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley: Chopped, for brightness.
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt: Enhances the flavors.
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper: For a mild kick.
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice: For a tangy zest.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare mushroom caps: Clean the mushroom caps and set aside. Chop the stems finely for the stuffing.
  3. Cook stuffing: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté the garlic and chopped mushroom stems for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add breadcrumbs and seasonings: Stir in the breadcrumbs, nutritional yeast, parsley, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Stuff the mushrooms: Fill each mushroom cap with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to pack it in.
  6. Bake: Place the stuffed mushroom caps on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the tops are golden and crispy.
  7. Serve: Allow to cool slightly before serving warm. Garnish with additional parsley if desired.
