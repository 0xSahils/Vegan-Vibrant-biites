Lentil Bolognese

This Lentil Bolognese is a mouthwatering vegan twist on the classic Italian pasta sauce, featuring tender lentils as the star ingredient. Simmered with ripe tomatoes, aromatic vegetables, and a medley of Italian herbs, this hearty sauce delivers rich, savory flavors in every bite. Perfectly paired with your favorite pasta, it’s a wholesome and comforting dish that’s packed with plant-based protein and fiber. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or preparing a cozy weeknight meal, this Lentil Bolognese is guaranteed to impress vegans and non-vegans alike. Serve it with fresh basil, vegan Parmesan, and crusty bread for the ultimate Italian feast.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing the vegetables.
  • 1 onion: Diced, for added flavor.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced, to enhance the aroma.
  • 2 medium carrots: Diced, for a subtle sweetness.
  • 2 celery stalks: Diced, for added texture and flavor.
  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils: Rinsed and drained.
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz): Adds a rich base to the sauce.
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste: To intensify the tomato flavor.
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable broth: (How to make vegan broth here).For the right consistency and flavor.
  • 1 tsp dried oregano: Adds a classic Italian flavor.
  • 1 tsp dried basil: Another staple herb for Italian dishes.
  • 1/2 tsp salt: Adjust to taste.
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper: Freshly ground for flavor.
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce: For umami depth.
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar: Adds a touch of acidity and sweetness.
  • Fresh parsley: Chopped, for garnish.
  • Pasta of choice: Spaghetti, penne, or any pasta you like.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Heat olive oil in a large skillet or saucepan over medium heat. Add the diced onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables have softened.
  2. Cook the lentils: Add the lentils to the pan along with the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, vegetable broth, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, soy sauce, and balsamic vinegar. Stir to combine.
  3. Simmer the sauce: Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer uncovered for 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender and the sauce has thickened.
  4. Cook the pasta: While the sauce is simmering, cook your pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Combine the pasta and sauce: Once the lentils are tender and the sauce is ready, toss the sauce with the cooked pasta, mixing well to coat the pasta in the rich lentil sauce.
  6. Serve: Divide the lentil Bolognese among plates, garnish with fresh parsley, and enjoy with your favorite vegan parmesan or nutritional yeast on top.
