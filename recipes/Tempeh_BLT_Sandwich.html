Tempeh BLT Sandwich

A mouthwatering plant-based twist on the classic BLT, the Vegan Tempeh BLT Sandwich delivers all the familiar flavors and textures you love in a healthier, cruelty-free package. Crispy, smoky tempeh slices are marinated and cooked to perfection, offering a satisfying and savory alternative to bacon. They’re layered with fresh, crisp lettuce, juicy, ripe tomatoes, and a generous spread of creamy vegan mayo, creating the ultimate combination of flavors. Sandwiched between two slices of perfectly toasted bread, this hearty and flavorful meal is as easy to make as it is to enjoy. Customize your sandwich with add-ons like avocado slices, caramelized onions, or a splash of hot sauce to elevate the experience even further. Whether you’re looking for a quick lunch, a casual dinner, or a portable option for picnics, this Tempeh BLT Sandwich is sure to impress. It’s a wholesome and satisfying dish that proves you don’t need meat to enjoy a truly great BLT. Packed with plant-based protein and vibrant ingredients, this sandwich is a must-try for vegans and non-vegans alike!

Ingredients

  • 1 block tempeh: Thinly sliced.
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce: For marinating the tempeh.
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup: Adds a touch of sweetness to the tempeh.
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika: For a smoky flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder: For seasoning.
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil: For frying the tempeh.
  • 4 slices bread: Choose your favorite type of bread, such as whole wheat or sourdough.
  • 1 cup lettuce: Washed and dried.
  • 1 tomato: Sliced.
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayo: For spreading on the bread.
  • 1 tablespoon mustard: Optional, for a tangy kick.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the tempeh: Slice the tempeh thinly and place it in a shallow bowl. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, maple syrup, smoked paprika, and garlic powder. Pour the marinade over the tempeh and let it sit for 10-15 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  2. Cook the tempeh: Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the marinated tempeh slices and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and crispy.
  3. Toast the bread: While the tempeh cooks, toast the bread slices to your desired level of crispiness.
  4. Assemble the sandwich: Spread vegan mayo (and mustard, if using) on one side of each toasted bread slice. On one slice, layer the crispy tempeh, followed by the lettuce and tomato slices.
  5. Serve: Place the second slice of toasted bread on top, mayo side down. Slice the sandwich in half and serve immediately. Enjoy your Tempeh BLT!
