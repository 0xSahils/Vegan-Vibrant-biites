Cauliflower Buffalo Wings

These Cauliflower Buffalo Wings are a flavorful and satisfying plant-based twist on a classic favorite. Made with tender cauliflower florets coated in a crispy batter and tossed in spicy buffalo sauce, they’re the ultimate comfort food with a healthy edge. Perfect as an appetizer, snack, or even a main dish, these wings deliver all the bold, tangy heat you crave without the need for meat. The cauliflower becomes beautifully golden and crisp in the oven, while the buffalo sauce adds a fiery kick that pairs perfectly with a cooling vegan ranch or blue cheese dip. Whether you’re hosting a game night, celebrating a party, or simply enjoying a cozy evening at home, these wings are guaranteed to impress vegans and non-vegans alike. Serve them with fresh celery and carrot sticks for a classic presentation, or enjoy them straight from the tray for a more casual treat. Packed with fiber and essential nutrients, these Cauliflower Buffalo Wings prove that indulgence and nutrition can go hand in hand. Once you try them, they’re sure to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce (vegan)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional for brushing)

    • Vegan Buffalo Sauce

    • 1/2 cup hot sauce (e.g., Frank’s RedHot)
    • 3 tbsp vegan butter (melted)
    • 1 tbsp maple syrup
    • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

    Whisk all ingredients together in a small bowl until fully combined. Use as a coating for cauliflower or as a dipping sauce.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 450°F (232°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare the cauliflower: Wash the cauliflower and cut it into bite-sized florets.
  3. Make the batter: In a large bowl, whisk together 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 1 cup of unsweetened plant-based milk, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp black pepper until smooth.
  4. Coat the cauliflower: Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, ensuring it’s fully coated, and place it on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake the cauliflower: Bake in the oven for 20 minutes, flipping the florets halfway through, until lightly golden and crisp.
  6. Make the buffalo sauce: While the cauliflower is baking, prepare the sauce by combining 1/2 cup of hot sauce (such as Frank’s RedHot), 3 tbsp vegan butter (melted), 1 tbsp maple syrup, and 1/2 tsp garlic powder in a bowl. Whisk until well combined.
  7. Toss with sauce: After 20 minutes, remove the cauliflower from the oven and toss the florets in the prepared buffalo sauce. Place them back on the baking sheet.
  8. Bake again: Return the coated cauliflower to the oven and bake for another 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce is caramelized and the edges are crispy.
  9. Serve: Let the cauliflower buffalo wings cool slightly before serving. Serve with vegan ranch or blue cheese dressing and celery sticks for a classic buffalo wings experience.
