Cauliflower Fried Rice

This Cauliflower Fried Rice is a vibrant and nutritious twist on the classic fried rice. Instead of using traditional rice, it’s made with cauliflower rice, which makes it a fantastic low-carb, gluten-free, and nutrient-packed option. The cauliflower rice is light yet filling, and it perfectly absorbs the flavors of the sautéed vegetables and savory seasonings. With colorful ingredients like peas, carrots, bell peppers, and onions, this dish is bursting with freshness and flavor. Whether you enjoy it as a quick and easy weeknight dinner or a side dish to complement your main course, this recipe is a crowd-pleaser. It’s a great way to sneak in extra veggies while still satisfying your cravings for comfort food. The addition of garlic, soy sauce (or tamari for a gluten-free option), and a dash of sesame oil adds that signature umami taste, while the scrambled tofu or vegan egg (optional) gives it a protein boost. Perfect for meal prepping, it’s a dish that can be made ahead and enjoyed throughout the week. Cauliflower Fried Rice is not only healthy but also customizable – you can add your favorite veggies or protein sources like tofu or tempeh to suit your tastes. Quick, easy, and full of flavor, it’s an excellent choice for those looking to eat healthy without compromising on taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower: Cut into florets and processed into rice-sized pieces.
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots: A quick and convenient addition to the dish.
  • 1 small onion: Diced.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced, for added flavor.
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce: Tamari or coconut aminos for gluten-free option.
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil: For flavor and sautéing the vegetables.
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil: For cooking the cauliflower rice and vegetables.
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger: To add a bit of warmth and spice.
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar: For acidity and balance.
  • 2 green onions: Chopped, for garnish.
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds: Optional, for garnish.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the cauliflower rice: Grate the cauliflower using a box grater or pulse the florets in a food processor until it resembles rice. Set aside.
  2. Cook the vegetables: Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil and sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add peas and carrots: Stir in the frozen peas and carrots and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until heated through.
  4. Cook the cauliflower rice: Push the vegetables to one side of the pan. Add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and the cauliflower rice to the empty side of the skillet. Sauté for 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cauliflower rice is tender and lightly browned.
  5. Combine everything: Stir the vegetables into the cauliflower rice. Add soy sauce, ginger, and rice vinegar. Stir well to combine and cook for another 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  6. Garnish and serve: Top with chopped green onions and sesame seeds before serving. Enjoy your healthy cauliflower fried rice!
