Vegan Cauliflower Alfredo

Indulge in this creamy and wholesome Vegan Cauliflower Alfredo sauce, a deliciously plant-based twist on the classic Alfredo. Made with tender cauliflower, garlic, and a blend of creamy plant-based ingredients, this sauce is rich, flavorful, and guilt-free. Perfect for drizzling over your favorite pasta or using as a base for other dishes, it's a satisfying and comforting meal that is both nutritious and indulgent.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium cauliflower: Cut into florets.
  • 2 tbsp olive oil: For a smooth texture.
  • 3 garlic cloves: Roasted or minced for flavor.
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk: To achieve creaminess.
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast: For a cheesy flavor.
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg: Optional, for added depth.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • Fresh parsley: Chopped, for garnish.
  • Your choice of pasta: Cooked according to package instructions.

Instructions

  1. Cook cauliflower: Begin by steaming or boiling the cauliflower florets until they become tender. This usually takes around 10-12 minutes. Once tender, drain the cauliflower and set it aside.
  2. Sauté garlic: In a large skillet, heat about 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté it for 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant and golden. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
  3. Blend ingredients: Transfer the cooked cauliflower, sautéed garlic, 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk, 2 tablespoons of nutritional yeast, a pinch of nutmeg, salt, and pepper into a blender or food processor. Blend everything until smooth and creamy. You may need to stop and scrape down the sides to ensure even blending.
  4. Adjust seasoning: Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning to your liking. If you want a richer flavor, add more nutritional yeast or a pinch of garlic powder. Add more salt or pepper if desired.
  5. Combine with pasta: Toss the creamy cauliflower Alfredo sauce with your choice of cooked pasta. Stir well to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
  6. Garnish: For a finishing touch, sprinkle some fresh chopped parsley on top of your dish. You can also add a sprinkle of vegan parmesan or a drizzle of olive oil if desired.
