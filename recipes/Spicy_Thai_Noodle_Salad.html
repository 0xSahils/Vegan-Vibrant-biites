Spicy Thai Noodle Salad

This Spicy Thai Noodle Salad is a vibrant, flavorful, and refreshing dish that perfectly balances spicy, tangy, and sweet flavors. Made with delicate rice noodles, this dish is packed with colorful vegetables like crunchy bell peppers, crisp carrots, and fresh cucumber, all tossed together in a creamy, spicy peanut dressing that is the star of the dish. The peanut dressing adds a rich, nutty flavor, complemented by a kick of heat from chili flakes or Sriracha sauce, and a hint of sweetness from maple syrup or coconut sugar. This salad is completely plant-based, making it a great choice for vegans, vegetarians, or anyone looking for a healthier meal option. The combination of textures — from the soft noodles to the crunchy vegetables — makes every bite exciting. This Thai-inspired salad is not only bursting with bold flavors but is also packed with essential nutrients, fiber, and antioxidants, making it a nutritious and satisfying meal. It’s perfect as a light lunch, dinner, or even as a side dish for your favorite Asian-inspired meals. The best part is that this dish can be customized to suit your preferences — you can add tofu, tempeh, or even edamame for extra protein, or swap out vegetables based on what you have on hand. With its combination of fresh ingredients and bold Thai flavors, this Spicy Thai Noodle Salad is sure to become a go-to meal for anyone craving something fresh, light, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 1 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1 cup julienned carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • Fried Tofu cubes How to make roasted Tofu
  • For the dressing:
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sriracha (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles: Prepare the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process.
  2. Prepare the vegetables: Chop and julienne the vegetables as listed, and set them aside.
  3. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, lime juice, sriracha, ginger, and garlic until smooth.
  4. Combine the ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, toss the cooked noodles, vegetables, and dressing until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Garnish and serve: Top the salad with chopped cilantro, green onions, and crushed peanuts. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 days.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Fiber: 5g
  • Sodium: 480mg
