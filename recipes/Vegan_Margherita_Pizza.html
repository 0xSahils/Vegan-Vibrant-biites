Vegan Margherita Pizza

Experience the delicious simplicity of this Vegan Margherita Pizza, a plant-based version of the classic that doesn’t compromise on flavor. With its crispy, golden crust, vibrant tomato sauce, and creamy vegan mozzarella, every bite is a burst of fresh, wholesome goodness. The addition of fragrant basil leaves elevates the dish, bringing a touch of elegance and authenticity to your dining table. This pizza is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a healthier alternative for those seeking to reduce dairy or follow a plant-based lifestyle. The crust is perfectly baked to provide a satisfying crunch, while the tomato sauce is seasoned with just the right balance of herbs and spices for a tangy kick. The vegan mozzarella melts beautifully, creating a creamy texture that ties the flavors together. Whether it’s for a quick weeknight dinner, a cozy weekend gathering, or even a celebration, this Vegan Margherita Pizza is versatile enough to fit any occasion. Pair it with a side salad or enjoy it on its own for a delightful, satisfying meal. Try it once, and you’ll find yourself coming back to this recipe whenever you crave a pizza that’s light, flavorful, and guilt-free!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough: Pre-made or homemade. (For homemade, use flour, yeast, olive oil, water, and salt.)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce: Preferably homemade or store-bought vegan pizza sauce.
  • 1 1/2 cups vegan mozzarella: Shredded.
  • 2 medium tomatoes: Sliced.
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves: Torn into pieces.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For drizzling.
  • Salt: To taste.
  • Black pepper: To taste.
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional): For extra flavor.
  • 1 tsp dried oregano: For a herby touch.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, preheat it as well.
  2. Prepare the dough: Roll out your pizza dough on a lightly floured surface into a round shape, about 12 inches in diameter.
  3. Assemble the pizza: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Sprinkle the shredded vegan mozzarella over the sauce.
  4. Add toppings: Arrange the tomato slices on top of the pizza, and sprinkle with garlic powder (if using), oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bake the pizza: Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or preheated pizza stone. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Finish with basil: Once out of the oven, drizzle the pizza with olive oil and scatter fresh basil leaves over the top. Slice and serve.
