Crispy Tofu Bites

These Crispy Tofu Bites are the perfect crunchy, savory snack. Whether you're looking for a quick appetizer or a delicious addition to any meal, these tofu bites will satisfy your cravings. Made with seasoned tofu, breaded, and baked to golden perfection, they are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. Plus, they’re vegan, gluten-free, and full of protein!

Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu: Pressed and cut into bite-sized cubes.
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs: Use gluten-free if needed.
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch: Helps create a crispy coating.
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce: For marinating.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: Adds crispiness during baking.
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder: For seasoning.
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder: For flavor.
  • 1/4 tsp paprika: Adds color and depth of flavor.
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper: For seasoning.
  • 1/4 tsp salt: Enhances all flavors.
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast: Adds a cheesy flavor (optional).

Instructions

  1. Prepare the tofu: Press the tofu for at least 15 minutes to remove excess water. Once pressed, cut it into bite-sized cubes.
  2. Marinate the tofu: In a bowl, mix soy sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, black pepper, salt, and nutritional yeast. Add the tofu cubes and toss to coat. Let marinate for 10 minutes.
  3. Prepare the breading: In a shallow dish, combine breadcrumbs and cornstarch.
  4. Bread the tofu: Coat each tofu cube in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing lightly to ensure it sticks.
  5. Bake the tofu: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the breaded tofu cubes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Serve: Remove the tofu from the oven and serve with your favorite dipping sauce, such as soy sauce or sweet chili sauce. Enjoy your crispy tofu bites!
