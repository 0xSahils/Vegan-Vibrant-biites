Vegan Pad See Ew

Vegan Pad See Ew is a flavorful and satisfying Thai stir-fry that features wide rice noodles, crispy tofu, and a savory blend of soy sauce, garlic, and vegetables. This plant-based twist on the traditional Pad See Ew is just as delicious, with smoky caramelized noodles and a touch of sweetness from the soy sauce, combined with crunchy greens like bok choy and broccoli. The tofu is pan-fried to crispy perfection, adding a nice contrast to the tender vegetables and noodles. The dish is finished off with a rich, tangy sauce that brings all the ingredients together in a harmonious and mouthwatering way. It’s a well-balanced meal that’s packed with protein, fiber, and a variety of fresh flavors. Vegan Pad See Ew is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or meal prep, as it comes together in just under 30 minutes. It's easily customizable—add in your favorite veggies or make it spicier with a dash of chili flakes. Whether you enjoy it with extra tofu, more veggies, or a side of chili sauce, this vegan Pad See Ew will bring the bold, comforting flavors of Thai cuisine right to your table. It’s a versatile and nourishing dish that’s both easy to make and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz rice noodles: Wide, flat noodles work best for this dish.
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil: For stir-frying the vegetables and noodles.
  • 1 small onion: Thinly sliced.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced.
  • 1 cup broccoli florets: Cut into bite-sized pieces.
  • 1 bell pepper: Thinly sliced.
  • 1 carrot: Julienned or sliced thinly.
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce: For the savory base flavor.
  • 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce: For depth of flavor and color.
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar: For a tangy kick.
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup: Adds a subtle sweetness.
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper: For seasoning.
  • 2 green onions: Chopped, for garnish.
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds: Optional, for garnish.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the rice noodles: Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry the vegetables: Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the onion and garlic, stir-frying until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add the vegetables: Add the broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot. Stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  4. Prepare the sauce: In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, dark soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, and black pepper.
  5. Combine noodles and sauce: Add the cooked rice noodles to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the sauce over the noodles and vegetables. Toss everything together and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine.
  6. Serve: Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds. Serve hot and enjoy your Vegan Pad See Ew!
