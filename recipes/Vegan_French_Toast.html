Vegan French Toast

Savor the comforting taste of classic French toast with this Vegan French Toast recipe. Made without eggs or dairy, this plant-based version features slices of bread dipped in a creamy, spiced batter crafted from plant-based milk, a touch of vanilla, and a sprinkle of cinnamon. Each piece is then perfectly pan-fried until golden brown, with a crisp exterior and a soft, fluffy center. Top your French toast with a drizzle of maple syrup, a dusting of powdered sugar, and a handful of fresh fruits like berries, bananas, or sliced apples for a breakfast that’s as beautiful as it is delicious. For added indulgence, pair it with a dollop of coconut whipped cream or a sprinkle of chopped nuts. This recipe is quick, easy, and customizable, making it ideal for lazy weekends or special brunches. Not only is it incredibly satisfying, but it’s also a great way to use up day-old bread while creating a delightful dish that everyone will love. Whether you’re a seasoned vegan or simply looking for a dairy-free and egg-free alternative, this Vegan French Toast is a delightful way to start your day on a sweet note.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plant-based milk: Such as almond or oat milk.
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed: Mixed with 6 tablespoons of water as an egg replacer.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract: For added flavor.
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon: Adds a warm spice note.
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup: Optional, for sweetness.
  • 6 slices of bread: Preferably thick and slightly stale.
  • Coconut oil or vegan butter: For cooking.
  • Toppings: Fresh fruits, syrup, and powdered sugar.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the batter: In a bowl, whisk together the plant-based milk, flaxseed mixture, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and maple syrup.
  2. Heat the pan: Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with coconut oil or vegan butter.
  3. Dip the bread: Submerge each slice of bread in the batter, coating both sides evenly.
  4. Cook the toast: Place the coated bread slices on the heated skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Serve: Plate the French toast and garnish with your favorite toppings. Enjoy!
