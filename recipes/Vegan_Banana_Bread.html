Vegan Banana Bread

Indulge in this creamy and garlicky Vegan Alfredo Pasta, made with a luscious, velvety cashew-based sauce that delivers all the richness and comfort of traditional Alfredo, without any dairy. The creamy cashew sauce is infused with roasted garlic, nutritional yeast, and a touch of lemon for a balanced, savory flavor profile. Tossed with perfectly cooked pasta, this dish offers a satisfying, comforting meal that’s perfect for cozy nights or when you're craving something indulgent. Whether you're a long-time vegan or just looking to try something new, this Vegan Alfredo Pasta is a crowd-pleaser that proves you don’t need dairy to enjoy a rich, flavorful pasta dish. Top with fresh herbs, a sprinkle of vegan parmesan, or a side of roasted vegetables for an added burst of flavor and texture. This dish is not only plant-based but also gluten-free (when paired with gluten-free pasta), making it a versatile and nourishing choice for everyone at the table.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas: Mashed.
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil: Melted.
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup: Or any other sweetener of choice.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract: For added flavor.
  • 1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour: Or all-purpose flour.
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda: For leavening.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt: Enhances the flavors.
  • 1/2 cup walnuts: Chopped, optional.
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips: Optional, for sweetness.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. Mix wet ingredients: In a large bowl, mash the bananas and mix with coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine dry ingredients: In another bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Combine wet and dry: Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients. Fold in walnuts and chocolate chips if using.
  5. Bake: Pour the batter into the loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool and serve: Let the banana bread cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
