Vegan Tofu Stir-Fry

This Vegan Tofu Stir-Fry is a vibrant and wholesome dish that’s bursting with flavor and color. Perfect for a quick and nutritious meal, it features crispy tofu cubes sautéed with a medley of fresh vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, and carrots. Tossed in a savory, slightly sweet sauce made with soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and a hint of sesame oil, this dish delivers a perfect balance of umami and freshness. Packed with plant-based protein from tofu and loaded with vitamins from the veggies, it’s a healthy, gluten-free meal that’s ready in just 30 minutes. Serve it over steamed rice, noodles, or quinoa for a satisfying and delicious dinner. This stir-fry is as versatile as it is flavorful, allowing you to customize the vegetables and spice levels to suit your taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 block (14 oz) firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup or agave
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Green onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the tofu: Press the tofu to remove excess moisture. Cube it into bite-sized pieces and toss with cornstarch for a crispy texture.
  2. Sauté the tofu: Heat olive oil in a skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Cook the vegetables: In the same skillet, add sesame oil and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant. Add the broccoli, bell peppers, and carrots, and cook for 5-7 minutes until slightly tender but still crisp.
  4. Make the sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, maple syrup, and vegetable broth. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and mix well.
  5. Combine tofu and vegetables: Return the tofu to the skillet and toss everything together, allowing the sauce to coat evenly. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Garnish and serve: Sprinkle with sesame seeds and green onions if desired. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

Let's Make a Colorful Meal!

This Vegan Tofu Stir-Fry is a feast for both the eyes and the taste buds! The bright red and yellow bell peppers, green broccoli, and orange carrots make this dish as vibrant as it is healthy. Cooking with colorful ingredients not only enhances the visual appeal of your meal but also provides a variety of essential nutrients to keep you energized.
