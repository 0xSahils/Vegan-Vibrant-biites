Crispy Baked Cauliflower

These crispy baked cauliflower bites are a healthy, flavorful alternative to fried cauliflower. Coated in a crunchy breadcrumb crust and baked to perfection, they make for a delicious plant-based snack or side dish. Perfect for dipping in your favorite vegan sauces, these cauliflower bites are sure to satisfy your craving for crispy comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium cauliflower head: Cut into florets.
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs: Use gluten-free if preferred.
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast: For cheesy flavor.
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder: Adds savory flavor.
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder: Adds depth to the flavor.
  • 1/2 tsp paprika: For a smoky taste.
  • 1/4 tsp salt: To taste.
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper: To taste.
  • 1/2 cup plant-based milk (e.g., almond milk): Helps the breadcrumbs stick.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For extra crispiness.
  • Optional: Hot sauce or vegan ranch for dipping: For added flavor.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare the cauliflower: Cut the cauliflower into bite-sized florets. Make sure they are roughly the same size to ensure even cooking.
  3. Make the coating: In a bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Dip the cauliflower: Dip each cauliflower floret into the plant-based milk, then coat it in the breadcrumb mixture. Press the breadcrumbs into the cauliflower to make sure it sticks.
  5. Arrange on the baking sheet: Place the coated cauliflower florets in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle olive oil over the top for extra crispiness.
  6. Bake: Bake for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the cauliflower is golden and crispy.
  7. Serve: Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce, such as vegan ranch or hot sauce. Enjoy!
