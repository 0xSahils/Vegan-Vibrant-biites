Vegan Brownies

These vegan brownies are the ultimate indulgence for anyone with a sweet tooth! Rich, fudgy, and decadent, they’re made without any dairy or eggs, making them the perfect treat for those following a plant-based diet. The combination of plant-based ingredients ensures that these brownies are not only vegan but also incredibly moist and full of flavor. With their dense texture and rich chocolatey taste, they are perfect for satisfying your dessert cravings or as a midday snack. The best part? You won’t miss the eggs or dairy—these brownies deliver all the indulgence you desire with none of the animal products. Whether served warm with a scoop of dairy-free ice cream or enjoyed on their own, these vegan brownies are sure to be a hit with everyone. A guilt-free dessert that doesn’t compromise on flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar or other sweetener of choice
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil or melted coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup dairy-free chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Begin by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease an 8x8-inch baking pan with a bit of oil, or you can line it with parchment paper for easy removal of the brownies after baking.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, coconut sugar, baking soda, and salt. Make sure everything is well combined to ensure the brownies have an even texture.
  3. Add the wet ingredients: Pour in the vegetable oil (or melted coconut oil), almond milk, and vanilla extract into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir the mixture until it becomes smooth, with no lumps or dry spots. The batter should be thick but pourable.
  4. Add chocolate chips: If you’re using dairy-free chocolate chips, gently fold them into the batter. This will give the brownies extra pockets of gooey chocolate when baked.
  5. Bake the brownies: Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan. Use a spatula to spread it out evenly so the brownies bake uniformly. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes. Check the brownies by inserting a toothpick in the center. If it comes out with a few moist crumbs (but not wet batter), they are done.
  6. Cool and serve: Once the brownies are done baking, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, cut into squares and serve. Enjoy your delicious vegan brownies!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 150
  • Protein: 2g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Fat: 6g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Sodium: 150mg
