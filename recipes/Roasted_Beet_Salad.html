Vegan Roasted Beet Salad

This Roasted Beet Salad is a vibrant, nutrient-packed dish that effortlessly combines earthy flavors with fresh, zesty notes. Featuring tender, oven-roasted beets as the star ingredient, this salad is a feast for both the eyes and the palate. The beets' natural sweetness is perfectly balanced by a tangy vinaigrette made with fresh citrus juice, a touch of Dijon mustard, and olive oil, creating a dressing that highlights the dish’s wholesome ingredients. The salad base is composed of crisp mixed greens, providing a refreshing and light foundation. To complement the beets, we’ve added crumbled vegan feta or goat cheese for creaminess, crunchy walnuts for texture, and thinly sliced red onions for a subtle bite. A handful of pomegranate seeds or orange segments can be included for a pop of fruity brightness, enhancing both the flavor and the visual appeal. This salad is not just delicious but also packed with essential nutrients like vitamins, antioxidants, and fiber, making it an excellent choice for a healthy lunch, dinner, or even as a show-stopping side dish at gatherings. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or preparing a quick weekday meal, this Roasted Beet Salad is sure to impress and delight your guests or family.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium beets (peeled and cut into cubes)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 4 cups mixed greens (such as arugula, spinach, or kale)
  • 1/4 cup walnuts (chopped, optional)
  • 1/4 cup vegan feta cheese (crumbled, optional)
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup olive oil (for dressing)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Roast the beets: Place the cubed beets on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Toss to coat evenly. Roast in the oven for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and slightly caramelized.
  3. Prepare the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Assemble the salad: In a large bowl, combine the roasted beets with the mixed greens. Add walnuts and vegan feta cheese if using.
  5. Dress the salad: Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine. Serve immediately.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 180
  • Protein: 3g
  • Carbohydrates: 22g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 5g
