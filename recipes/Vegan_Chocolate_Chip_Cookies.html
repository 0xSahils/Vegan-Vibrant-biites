Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

These Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies are a plant-based twist on a timeless classic, offering everything you love about cookies—chewy centers, crisp edges, and melty chocolate goodness. Made without any dairy or eggs, these cookies are perfect for anyone looking to enjoy a delicious treat while keeping it vegan. The dough is crafted from simple, wholesome ingredients like all-purpose or whole-grain flour, coconut or brown sugar for a rich caramel-like sweetness, and a touch of vanilla extract for depth of flavor. Instead of butter, these cookies use plant-based oil or vegan butter, creating the perfect texture and a hint of richness. A splash of plant-based milk ensures the dough comes together smoothly. What truly makes these cookies shine are the generous chunks of dairy-free chocolate chips, which melt into gooey perfection with every bite. Whether enjoyed warm out of the oven or cooled with a glass of almond milk, these cookies are the ultimate comfort food. Perfect for sharing with friends, serving at gatherings, or simply enjoying as an afternoon snack, these Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies are sure to become a household favorite. Easy to make and utterly satisfying, they’re proof that you don’t need animal products to create the perfect cookie!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter (or coconut oil)
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients: In a bowl, combine vegan butter, almond milk, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.
  3. Combine the dry ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together flour, coconut sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Form the dough: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop and bake: Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto the baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  6. Cool: Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Nutrition Information (per cookie)

  • Calories: 150
  • Protein: 2g
  • Carbohydrates: 20g
  • Fat: 8g
  • Fiber: 2g
