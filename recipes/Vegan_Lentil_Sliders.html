Vegan Lentil Sliders

These Vegan Lentil Sliders are a delicious and satisfying plant-based alternative to traditional burgers. Made with hearty lentils, oats, and a blend of savory spices, these sliders are packed with flavor and protein. The lentils are cooked to perfection and then combined with aromatic onions, garlic, and spices like cumin and paprika for a savory bite that will satisfy your taste buds. Each slider is topped with fresh veggies such as lettuce, tomato, and avocado, making them not only tasty but also nutrient-packed. Serve them on soft, toasted buns with your favorite condiments like vegan mayo, ketchup, or mustard for the ultimate vegan burger experience. These sliders are easy to make, perfect for meal prep, and a great option for parties, barbecues, or family dinners. They can also be customized with different toppings, sauces, and even a vegan cheese slice for extra flavor. Whether you're craving a delicious vegan lunch or a savory dinner, these Lentil Sliders are sure to become a favorite in your meal rotation. They're a great way to enjoy plant-based eating without compromising on taste or satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked lentils: Any variety will work well.
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs: Use gluten-free if needed.
  • 1 small onion: Finely chopped.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced.
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin: Adds depth of flavor.
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika: For a smoky touch.
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce: Or tamari for gluten-free option.
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley: Chopped.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • Slider buns: Choose your favorite vegan buns.
  • Toppings: Lettuce, tomato slices, and vegan mayo.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the mixture: In a mixing bowl, combine cooked lentils, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, cumin, smoked paprika, soy sauce, and parsley. Mix well and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Form patties: Shape the mixture into small slider-sized patties.
  3. Cook the patties: Heat a skillet over medium heat with a little oil. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the sliders: Place the cooked patties on slider buns and top with lettuce, tomato slices, and vegan mayo.
  5. Serve: Enjoy your Vegan Lentil Sliders warm and fresh!
