Vegan BBQ Tacos

These Vegan BBQ Tacos are bursting with smoky, tangy flavors that will make your taste buds dance! The hearty plant-based filling is coated in a rich BBQ sauce, offering a perfect balance of sweetness and smokiness. Topped with crunchy, fresh vegetables like lettuce, tomatoes, and onions, each bite is a delightful combination of textures and tastes. A creamy sauce ties everything together, adding a smooth, indulgent touch to this satisfying meal. Whether enjoyed as a quick lunch, dinner, or even at a party, these BBQ tacos are sure to be a crowd-pleaser. They're fully customizable, so feel free to add your favorite toppings or switch up the filling for a unique twist. Perfect for anyone craving a flavorful, plant-based meal with all the deliciousness of classic BBQ!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jackfruit: Shredded.
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce: Your favorite vegan brand.
  • 8 small tortillas: Warmed.
  • 1 cup coleslaw mix: For topping.
  • 1 avocado: Sliced.
  • Fresh cilantro: Chopped, for garnish.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For cooking.
  • 1 lime: Cut into wedges.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the jackfruit: Begin by heating 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the shredded jackfruit (you can either use canned young jackfruit in brine or fresh jackfruit if available). Sauté the jackfruit for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure even cooking. The jackfruit will start to soften and take on a slightly crispy texture on the edges, mimicking pulled pork.
  2. Add BBQ sauce: Pour in your favorite BBQ sauce (around 1/2 to 3/4 cup, depending on how saucy you want it). Stir to coat the jackfruit evenly. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. This allows the jackfruit to absorb the smoky, tangy BBQ flavors. If you prefer a more intense flavor, let it simmer for an additional few minutes. You may add a splash of water or vegetable broth if it starts to dry out.
  3. Assemble the tacos: While the jackfruit is simmering, warm the tortillas in a separate pan or microwave for about 30 seconds to make them more pliable. Once warmed, place each tortilla on a plate. Spoon the BBQ jackfruit mixture evenly across the center of each tortilla. Add a generous handful of coleslaw mix on top of the jackfruit, followed by a few slices of creamy avocado to add richness and balance the flavors.
  4. Garnish: Garnish your tacos with fresh cilantro leaves for a burst of freshness. Squeeze a wedge of lime over the top to add a zesty, tangy kick that will enhance all the flavors in the taco.
  5. Serve: Serve the tacos immediately while they’re still warm, and enjoy the smoky, savory flavors of the BBQ jackfruit combined with the crunch of the slaw and the creaminess of the avocado. These tacos are perfect for a quick lunch, dinner, or a fun meal to share with friends and family!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 300
  • Protein: 5g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Fiber: 6g
