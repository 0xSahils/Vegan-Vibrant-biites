Sweet Potato Tacos

These Vegan Sweet Potato Tacos are a flavorful and wholesome twist on a classic favorite, perfect for any meal or gathering. The roasted sweet potatoes, caramelized to perfection with a hint of smoky spices, provide a hearty and satisfying base. Layered with vibrant and fresh toppings like crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and creamy avocado, every bite bursts with contrasting textures and flavors. What sets these tacos apart is the tangy lime dressing that ties all the ingredients together, adding a zesty kick to the warm tortillas. This dish is entirely plant-based, making it a nutritious and crowd-pleasing option for vegans and non-vegans alike. Customize your tacos with your favorite toppings, such as pickled onions, fresh cilantro, or a sprinkle of vegan cheese, to create a meal that suits your taste. Perfect for busy weeknights or weekend gatherings, these tacos are easy to prepare and irresistibly delicious. Whether you’re hosting a taco night or simply craving a satisfying meal, these Vegan Sweet Potato Tacos are guaranteed to impress and leave everyone coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes (peeled and diced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1/2 cup red cabbage (shredded)
  • 1/4 cup cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 lime (cut into wedges)
  • 1/4 cup vegan sour cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Roast the sweet potatoes: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the diced sweet potatoes with olive oil, smoked paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Spread them in a single layer on a baking sheet. Roast for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and slightly caramelized.
  2. Prepare the toppings: While the sweet potatoes are roasting, shred the red cabbage, chop the cilantro, and mashed avocado (or can slice it too). Set aside.
  3. Warm the tortillas: Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for 1-2 minutes on each side, until warm and pliable.
  4. Assemble the tacos: Once the sweet potatoes are roasted, assemble the tacos by placing a generous scoop of sweet potatoes in each tortilla. Top with shredded cabbage, avocado slices, cilantro, and a dollop of vegan sour cream (optional).
  5. Serve: Serve the tacos with lime wedges on the side for extra zest. Enjoy!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 220
  • Protein: 4g
  • Carbohydrates: 34g
  • Fat: 9g
  • Fiber: 7g
