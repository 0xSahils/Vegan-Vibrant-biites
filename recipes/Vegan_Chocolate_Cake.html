Vegan Chocolate Cake

Indulge in this rich and decadent Vegan Chocolate Cake that’s perfect for any occasion. Made without any dairy or eggs, this cake is moist, fluffy, and bursting with deep chocolate flavor. The combination of cocoa powder and plant-based milk creates a smooth, velvety texture, while the use of simple ingredients like flour, sugar, and baking soda ensures a light and airy cake. The magic of this cake lies in its ability to capture the essence of traditional chocolate cake while remaining completely plant-based. The sweetness comes from coconut sugar or your favorite sweetener, and the richness is enhanced by the addition of a splash of vanilla extract. A dash of apple cider vinegar is added to activate the baking soda, ensuring the cake rises beautifully and achieves the perfect crumb. Once baked, this vegan chocolate cake is perfect on its own or dressed up with your favorite frosting. Whether you prefer a creamy vegan buttercream, a rich chocolate ganache, or a simple dusting of powdered sugar, this cake can be customized to suit your tastes. The result is a dessert that’s sure to impress, whether for a birthday celebration, a special occasion, or simply as a treat to enjoy with loved ones. Enjoy a slice of this guilt-free vegan chocolate cake and savor every bite of its deep, chocolaty goodness!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips (optional, for added richness)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Combine wet ingredients: In a separate bowl, mix together almond milk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and apple cider vinegar.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients: Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Add boiling water: Gradually stir in the boiling water until the batter is smooth. The batter will be thin.
  6. Optional chocolate chips: Fold in the vegan chocolate chips for an extra chocolatey flavor.
  7. Bake the cake: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool the cakes: Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Frost the cake: Once the cakes are completely cool, frost with your favorite vegan chocolate frosting and serve!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 3g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Sodium: 220mg
