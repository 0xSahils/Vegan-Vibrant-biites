Vegan Sushi Rolls

These Vegan Sushi Rolls are a fun and flavorful way to enjoy sushi without any fish or animal products. Packed with fresh vegetables like cucumber, avocado, and carrots, these rolls are a vibrant and healthy choice. The combination of slightly sweet rice, tangy rice vinegar, and crispy nori provides the perfect balance of flavors and textures. You can easily customize the filling by adding your favorite veggies, such as bell peppers, sprouts, or even marinated tofu for a protein-packed twist. The rolls are easy to make, and with the help of a bamboo mat, you can roll them up tightly and slice them into bite-sized pieces. Serve with a side of soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for an authentic sushi experience. These vegan sushi rolls are not only delicious but also a great way to add more plant-based ingredients into your diet. They’re perfect for a light lunch, dinner, or even as a fun appetizer for a party. Plus, they’re naturally gluten-free when you use tamari soy sauce, making them suitable for various dietary needs. Whether you’re a sushi lover or new to making sushi at home, these vegan rolls are sure to impress with their vibrant colors, fresh ingredients, and delightful flavors. Perfect for those looking for a quick, healthy, and satisfying meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, julienned
  • Soy sauce or tamari for dipping
  • Pickled ginger (optional)
  • Wasabi (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sushi Rice: Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear. In a saucepan, combine the rice and water. Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for another 10 minutes. In a small bowl, combine rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Stir until dissolved, then gently mix the vinegar mixture into the cooked rice. Let the rice cool to room temperature.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables: While the rice is cooling, slice the avocado, cucumber, carrot, and red bell pepper into thin strips. Keep them separate and ready for assembly.
  3. Assemble the Sushi Rolls: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down. Using your fingers, spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top. Make sure the rice is not packed too tightly. Arrange the avocado, cucumber, carrot, and bell pepper strips in the center of the rice.
  4. Roll the Sushi: Carefully lift the edge of the sushi mat closest to you and roll it over the fillings, using your fingers to gently press and shape the roll as you go. When you reach the exposed border of the nori, moisten it with a little water and press to seal the roll.
  5. Cut the Rolls: Use a sharp knife to slice the sushi roll into 6-8 pieces. Clean the knife between cuts to prevent it from sticking to the rice.
  6. Serve: Arrange the sushi rolls on a plate, serve with soy sauce or tamari for dipping, and add pickled ginger and wasabi on the side for extra flavor. Enjoy your delicious vegan sushi rolls!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 5g
  • Carbohydrates: 44g
  • Fat: 9g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Sodium: 600mg
