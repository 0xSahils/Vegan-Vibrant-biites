Vegan Chocolate Mousse

Treat yourself to the ultimate dessert experience with this rich and velvety Vegan Chocolate Mousse. Crafted with wholesome, plant-based ingredients, this mousse delivers all the indulgence of a traditional recipe without any dairy or eggs. The secret to its creamy, airy texture lies in the use of aquafaba (chickpea water) or coconut cream, which is whipped to perfection and folded into a luscious blend of melted dark chocolate and natural sweeteners. Each spoonful offers a decadent chocolate flavor that melts in your mouth, making it an irresistible treat for chocolate lovers. This mousse is not only dairy-free and egg-free but also gluten-free, making it an excellent choice for those with dietary restrictions. It’s easy to prepare and requires only a handful of ingredients, yet it feels luxurious enough for special occasions or dinner parties. Serve it in elegant glasses, topped with fresh berries, shaved chocolate, or a dollop of vegan whipped cream for a stunning presentation. Whether you’re celebrating a milestone or simply craving something sweet, this Vegan Chocolate Mousse is guaranteed to impress and satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400ml) coconut cream: Chilled overnight.
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips: Dairy-free.
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup: For sweetness.
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract: Adds flavor depth.
  • Pinch of salt: Enhances the chocolate flavor.

Instructions

  1. Chill coconut cream: Refrigerate overnight and scoop out the thick cream.
  2. Melt chocolate: Gently melt the chocolate chips in a double boiler or microwave.
  3. Whisk coconut cream: In a bowl, whip the chilled coconut cream until light and fluffy.
  4. Add ingredients: Gradually fold in melted chocolate, maple syrup, vanilla, and a pinch of salt.
  5. Chill mousse: Divide the mixture into serving glasses and refrigerate for 2 hours or until set.
  6. Garnish: Top with fresh berries, shaved chocolate, or a sprig of mint before serving.
