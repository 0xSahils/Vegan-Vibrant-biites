Crispy Roasted Tofu

Crispy Roasted Tofu is a simple yet delicious dish that transforms tofu into a flavorful and satisfying meal. Marinated in a savory blend of soy sauce, garlic, and spices, the tofu is roasted until golden brown and crispy on the outside while remaining tender inside. This versatile dish pairs perfectly with salads, grain bowls, or as a protein-packed snack on its own.

Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu (pressed and cubed)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the tofu: Press the tofu to remove excess water, then cut it into bite-sized cubes.
  3. Marinate: In a bowl, mix soy sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, and black pepper. Toss the tofu cubes in the marinade and let sit for 10 minutes.
  4. Coat with cornstarch: Sprinkle cornstarch over the tofu and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Roast: Arrange the tofu on a lined baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve: Enjoy as a main dish, in salads, or with rice and vegetables.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 150
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 6g
  • Fat: 8g
  • Fiber: 1g
