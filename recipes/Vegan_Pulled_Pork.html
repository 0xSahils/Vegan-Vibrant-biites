Vegan Pulled Pork

Savor the smoky, sweet, and tangy flavors of Vegan Pulled Pork, made with tender, shredded jackfruit. This plant-based alternative to the classic dish perfectly mimics the texture of pulled pork while delivering a delicious and satisfying meal. Coated in a rich barbecue sauce, the jackfruit absorbs all the smoky and savory flavors, making it an irresistible centerpiece for sandwiches, tacos, or sliders. Serve it with creamy coleslaw, pickles, and a side of fries for a classic barbecue-style meal or get creative with your own accompaniments. The versatility of this recipe makes it perfect for casual weeknight dinners, summer cookouts, or even potluck gatherings. Jackfruit, a nutrient-rich fruit, is a great source of fiber and vitamins, making this dish not only tasty but also wholesome. Whether you're a seasoned vegan or exploring plant-based eating, this Vegan Pulled Pork is sure to impress with its bold flavors and hearty texture. Quick to prepare and easy to customize, it’s a dish that will quickly become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans young jackfruit in brine: The star ingredient, shredded for a pulled pork texture.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing the jackfruit and spices.
  • 1 onion, finely chopped: Adds a savory base to the dish.
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced: Enhances the flavor profile.
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika: Provides a smoky flavor essential for barbecue dishes.
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin: Adds depth and warmth to the dish.
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder: For a mild heat kick.
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce: Use your favorite vegan BBQ sauce for authentic flavor.
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth: (How to make Vegetable Broth)To keep the jackfruit moist and flavorful.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • Burger buns: For serving.
  • Optional toppings: Coleslaw, pickles, or extra BBQ sauce.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the jackfruit: Drain and rinse the jackfruit. Use your hands or a fork to shred the jackfruit into small, pulled pieces.
  2. Sauté the aromatics: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add the spices: Sprinkle in the smoked paprika, cumin, and chili powder. Stir well to coat the onions and garlic.
  4. Cook the jackfruit: Add the shredded jackfruit to the skillet. Stir to combine with the spices and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the sauce: Pour in the barbecue sauce and vegetable broth. Mix well and let the jackfruit simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Assemble and serve: Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. Serve the jackfruit on burger buns with your choice of toppings. Enjoy!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 4g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fat: 8g
  • Fiber: 6g
