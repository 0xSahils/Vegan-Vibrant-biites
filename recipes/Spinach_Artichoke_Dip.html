Spinach Artichoke Dip

This creamy and savory Spinach Artichoke Dip is a perfect indulgence for any occasion, whether you're hosting a party, preparing for a family gathering, or just craving a comforting snack. Made with rich cashews and velvety vegan cream cheese, this dip delivers the same beloved, cheesy texture and flavor as the traditional version, but with no dairy. The hearty combination of tender spinach, artichokes, and a blend of spices creates a deliciously balanced dip that’s both savory and satisfying. Cashews lend a creamy richness that perfectly complements the earthiness of spinach and the tang of artichokes. Whether you serve it with crunchy crackers, warm pita bread, or fresh veggies like carrots and celery, every bite is guaranteed to be full of flavor and texture. This vegan-friendly version makes it a great option for those with dairy restrictions or anyone looking for a healthier alternative. With its irresistible flavors and creamy texture, this Spinach Artichoke Dip is bound to become a crowd favorite. It’s easy to make, can be served warm or cold, and is a great addition to any snack table, potluck, or game day spread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw cashews: Soaked for 4 hours or overnight, this provides a creamy base.
  • 1 cup canned artichoke hearts: Drained and chopped.
  • 2 cups fresh spinach: Chopped, for the spinach flavor and nutrition.
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast: For a cheesy, nutty flavor.
  • 1/4 cup vegan cream cheese: Adds a rich, creamy texture.
  • 1/4 cup plant-based milk: Any plant-based milk, such as almond or soy milk.
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice: For a tangy flavor that balances the richness.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced, for added flavor.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the cashew cream: Drain the soaked cashews and place them in a blender or food processor with the plant-based milk, lemon juice, and nutritional yeast. Blend until smooth and creamy. Set aside.
  2. Cook the spinach: In a pan, heat a little oil and sauté the garlic until fragrant. Add the spinach and cook until wilted, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. Combine the ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cashew cream, sautéed spinach, chopped artichoke hearts, and vegan cream cheese. Mix until smooth and well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Bake the dip: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Transfer the dip to an oven-safe dish and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden on top.
  5. Serve: Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy with your favorite crackers, bread, or fresh veggies!
