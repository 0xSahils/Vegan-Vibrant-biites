Vegan Breakfast Burritos

These Vegan Breakfast Burritos are the perfect way to kickstart your day! Filled with scrambled tofu, sautéed vegetables, creamy avocado, and a zesty salsa, all wrapped in a soft, warm tortilla. The scrambled tofu is a savory stand-in for eggs, while the sautéed veggies add a burst of flavor and texture. The avocado brings a creamy richness, and the salsa adds just the right amount of tang. Not only are these burritos packed with protein and healthy fats, but they are also incredibly satisfying and great for meal prep. Whether you’re looking for a hearty breakfast to fuel your day or a quick meal to enjoy during busy mornings, these breakfast burritos are the ideal choice. They’re also perfect for a leisurely weekend brunch with family or friends. Make a batch ahead of time and have a ready-to-go, flavorful meal whenever you need it. These burritos are customizable too—add your favorite toppings or switch up the veggies to suit your taste!

Ingredients

  • 4 large tortillas: Soft, whole wheat or flour tortillas.
  • 1 block firm tofu: Crumbled, for the scrambled "eggs" filling.
  • 1 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing the veggies and tofu.
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers: Diced, for a colorful crunch.
  • 1/2 cup onions: Diced, for a savory flavor.
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms: Sliced, for added texture and flavor.
  • 1/4 cup spinach: Fresh, chopped spinach for added nutrients.
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast: For a cheesy, nutty flavor.
  • 1 tsp garlic powder: For seasoning the tofu scramble.
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder: For color and flavor.
  • Salt and pepper: To taste.
  • 1 avocado: Sliced, for creamy texture.
  • 1/2 cup salsa: For a tangy and spicy kick.
  • 1 tbsp fresh cilantro: Chopped, for garnish.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the tofu: Press the tofu to remove excess moisture, then crumble it into small pieces using your hands or a fork.
  2. Cook the vegetables: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and spinach. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until softened.
  3. Scramble the tofu: Add the crumbled tofu to the skillet with the vegetables. Stir in the garlic powder, turmeric, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally until the tofu is golden and heated through.
  4. Assemble the burritos: Warm the tortillas in a separate pan or microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable. Spoon a generous amount of the tofu mixture into the center of each tortilla.
  5. Add the toppings: Top with sliced avocado, salsa, and fresh cilantro.
  6. Wrap it up: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly into a burrito.
  7. Serve: Cut in half and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious Vegan Breakfast Burritos!
