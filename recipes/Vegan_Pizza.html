Vegan Pizza

Indulge in a delicious Vegan Pizza that’s bursting with flavors and textures. This plant-based version of the classic favorite features a crispy, golden crust topped with a rich tomato sauce, gooey vegan cheese, and a medley of colorful vegetables. Customize your pizza with toppings like mushrooms, bell peppers, red onions, olives, cherry tomatoes, and fresh spinach for a vibrant and satisfying meal. The beauty of vegan pizza lies in its versatility—whether you prefer a classic Margherita style with fresh basil and olive oil or a loaded veggie delight with an array of seasonal produce, the possibilities are endless. Pair the pizza with a drizzle of vegan pesto or balsamic glaze for an extra touch of flavor. Made with wholesome ingredients, this pizza is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, making it a guilt-free indulgence. Perfect for weeknight dinners, gatherings, or even a fun DIY pizza night, this recipe is sure to please vegans and non-vegans alike. Enjoy the irresistible combination of crispy crust, tangy sauce, and savory toppings that make this Vegan Pizza a crowd-pleaser every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup vegan mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare the pizza dough: If using store-bought dough, follow the package instructions. If making homemade dough, roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Add tomato sauce: Spread a generous layer of tomato sauce over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Top the pizza: Sprinkle the shredded vegan mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add the sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, and olives. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with dried oregano and red pepper flakes, if using.
  5. Bake the pizza: Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Garnish and serve: Remove the pizza from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Slice and enjoy!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 280
  • Protein: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 32g
  • Fat: 14g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Sodium: 450mg
