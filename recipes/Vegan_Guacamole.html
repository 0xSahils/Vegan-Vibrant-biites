Vegan Guacamole

This vegan guacamole is the ultimate crowd-pleaser. Made with ripe avocados, zesty lime, and a blend of fresh ingredients, it’s creamy, tangy, and packed with flavor. Serve it with tortilla chips, as a sandwich spread, or alongside your favorite dishes.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 Roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 jalapeño, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the avocados: Slice the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a large bowl.
  2. Mash the avocados: Use a fork or potato masher to mash the avocados until you reach your desired consistency.
  3. Add the ingredients: Mix in lime juice, red onion, tomatoes, jalapeño, cilantro, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined.
  4. Taste and adjust: Adjust seasoning as needed, adding more lime juice, salt, or pepper to taste.
  5. Serve: Transfer the guacamole to a serving bowl and garnish with extra cilantro or a sprinkle of chili flakes. Enjoy immediately.

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 150
  • Protein: 2g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fat: 13g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Sodium: 200mg
