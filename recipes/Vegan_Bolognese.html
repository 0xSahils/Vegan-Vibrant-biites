Vegan Bolognese

This vegan Bolognese is a hearty, comforting sauce made with lentils, tomatoes, and a medley of vegetables. It's a plant-based twist on the classic Italian recipe, perfect for serving over pasta or as a topping for lasagna. The lentils provide a rich, meaty texture, while the tomatoes offer a savory base, and the vegetables add layers of flavor. Packed with protein and fiber, this dish is not only delicious but also nutritious. Whether you’re craving a cozy dinner or preparing a meal for a crowd, this vegan Bolognese is sure to satisfy even the biggest fans of traditional Bolognese. It’s easy to make, customizable, and full of flavor, making it a perfect addition to your plant-based meal repertoire. Enjoy it with your favorite pasta or serve it as a filling for stuffed vegetables or a savory pie. With its rich taste and satisfying texture, this vegan Bolognese proves that plant-based meals can be just as indulgent and comforting as their traditional counterparts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green or brown lentils: Rinsed and drained.
  • 2 tbsp olive oil: For sautéing the vegetables.
  • 1 large onion: Finely chopped.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced.
  • 2 large carrots: Diced.
  • 2 celery stalks: Diced.
  • 1 zucchini: Diced.
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) crushed tomatoes: For the base of the sauce.
  • 1 cup vegetable broth: (How to make vegan broth here).To help simmer the sauce.
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste: For a richer tomato flavor.
  • 1 tsp dried oregano: Adds an herbaceous flavor.
  • 1 tsp dried basil: For an Italian touch.
  • 1/2 tsp salt: To taste.
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper: To taste.
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce or tamari: Adds umami flavor.
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar: For a touch of acidity.
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley: Chopped, for garnish.
  • Optional: 1 cup cooked pasta: For serving.

Instructions

  1. Cook the lentils: In a medium pot, cook the lentils in water or vegetable broth for about 20-25 minutes, or until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the vegetables: Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, celery, and zucchini. Cook, stirring occasionally, for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add the tomato ingredients: Stir in the crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, soy sauce, and balsamic vinegar. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Simmer the sauce: Add the cooked lentils to the sauce and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine. Stir occasionally, and add more broth if needed.
  5. Serve: Serve the vegan Bolognese sauce over pasta or use it as a topping for lasagna. Garnish with fresh parsley and enjoy!
