Roasted Cauliflower Steaks

Roasted cauliflower steaks are a flavorful and hearty vegan dish that’s perfect for both a main course or a side. The cauliflower is carefully sliced into thick "steaks," seasoned with a delicious blend of olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and a mix of aromatic spices, then roasted to golden perfection. This simple yet satisfying dish brings out the natural sweetness of the cauliflower, while the spices add depth and complexity to each bite. The crispy edges and tender, flavorful center make these roasted cauliflower steaks a delightful choice for anyone looking for a satisfying, plant-based option. They are not only easy to prepare but also versatile, as they can be paired with a variety of sides such as quinoa, rice, or roasted vegetables. For an extra touch of richness, serve with a drizzle of tahini sauce or a sprinkle of fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro. These roasted cauliflower steaks are also packed with nutrients, including fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, making them a healthy addition to any meal. Whether you serve them as a main dish for a light dinner or as a side to complement a more hearty meal, they are sure to impress with their savory flavor and satisfying texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 large cauliflower: Cut into thick slices (steaks).
  • 3 tbsp olive oil: For roasting.
  • 2 cloves garlic: Minced.
  • 1 tsp paprika: Adds smoky flavor.
  • 1/2 tsp cumin: For earthy warmth.
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric: For a pop of color.
  • Salt: To taste.
  • Black pepper: To taste.
  • 1 lemon: Zested and juiced for brightness.
  • Fresh parsley: Chopped, for garnish.
  • Red pepper flakes (optional): For heat.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the cauliflower: Cut the cauliflower into 1-inch thick slices (steaks). You may have some smaller florets left over, which can be roasted alongside the steaks or used in another dish.
  3. Season the cauliflower: In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, garlic, paprika, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Brush this mixture onto both sides of each cauliflower steak.
  4. Roast the cauliflower: Place the cauliflower steaks on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Roast for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and tender.
  5. Finish with lemon: Once the steaks are roasted, drizzle with lemon juice and zest. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and red pepper flakes (if using) for extra flavor.
  6. Serve: Serve the roasted cauliflower steaks hot, as a main or side dish, with your favorite dipping sauce or a grain like quinoa or rice.
