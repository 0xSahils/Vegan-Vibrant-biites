Vegan Burrito Bowl

This Vegan Burrito Bowl is a colorful and customizable meal that brings together a variety of textures and flavors. With a base of hearty grains like quinoa or rice, it’s topped with protein-packed black beans, sweet corn, and fresh veggies like bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and red onion. The creamy avocado slices and tangy lime dressing elevate the dish, making each bite a perfect balance of savory and fresh. Whether you're craving something mild or looking to spice it up, this burrito bowl can be tailored to your liking with different toppings like vegan cheese, sour cream, and hot sauce. It’s a versatile, nutritious, and satisfying meal perfect for lunch, dinner, or meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or rice
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), diced
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Vegan sour cream (optional)
  • Vegan cheese (optional)
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Base: Start by cooking 1 cup of quinoa or rice according to the package instructions. Once cooked, fluff with a fork and place a generous layer of quinoa or rice at the bottom of a large bowl. This serves as the hearty base for your burrito bowl, providing a satisfying foundation.
  2. Add the Beans and Veggies: On top of the base, layer 1 cup of black beans (either canned or cooked) and 1/2 cup of corn kernels. Then, add diced bell pepper (any color), finely chopped red onion, and halved cherry tomatoes for a burst of color and flavor. These veggies not only add texture but also bring freshness to the bowl.
  3. Prepare the Avocado: Slice 1 ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh. Cut it into slices and arrange them neatly on top of the other ingredients. The avocado will add a creamy texture and rich flavor that perfectly complements the other elements of the dish.
  4. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, combine the juice of 1 lime, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 teaspoon of cumin, 1 teaspoon of chili powder, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Whisk the ingredients together until well-combined. This dressing will give your burrito bowl a tangy and zesty kick, enhancing the flavors.
  5. Top and Serve: Drizzle the dressing evenly over the ingredients in the bowl. For added flavor, top with a sprinkle of fresh cilantro, a dollop of vegan sour cream, and a sprinkle of vegan cheese. If you prefer some heat, drizzle with your favorite hot sauce or salsa. Serve immediately and enjoy the vibrant, colorful, and delicious meal!

Nutrition Information (per serving)

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 10g
  • Sodium: 350mg
