Vegan Mac And Cheese

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this creamy and delicious Vegan Mac and Cheese. Made with a rich, velvety sauce crafted from cashews, nutritional yeast, and plant-based milk, this dish delivers all the cheesy goodness you love—without any dairy. The sauce is infused with garlic, onion, and a hint of mustard for a depth of flavor that perfectly complements tender elbow macaroni. This vegan twist on a classic favorite is perfect for weeknight dinners, family gatherings, or whenever you crave a bowl of warm, cheesy comfort. It’s a kid-approved meal that’s as wholesome as it is indulgent, featuring nutrient-rich ingredients and no artificial additives. Customize your Vegan Mac and Cheese by adding your favorite mix-ins like steamed broccoli, sautéed mushrooms, or crispy vegan bacon. Garnish with fresh parsley or a sprinkle of smoked paprika for an extra layer of flavor. Whether you're new to plant-based eating or a seasoned vegan, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings for creamy, cheesy pasta. It’s easy to make, endlessly customizable, and guaranteed to become a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • For the Sauce:
  • 1 cup raw cashews (soaked in hot water for 15-20 minutes)
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened plant-based milk (e.g., almond, soy, or oat)
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or melted vegan butter
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional, for added flavor)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Pasta:
  • 12 ounces elbow macaroni or your favorite pasta (gluten-free, if needed)
  • Optional Toppings and Add-ins:
  • Steamed broccoli florets
  • Sautéed mushrooms
  • Crispy vegan bacon bits
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • Sprinkle of smoked paprika

Instructions

  1. Soak the cashews: Place the cashews in a bowl and cover them with hot water. Let them soak for 15-20 minutes to soften. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Blend the sauce: In a blender, combine the soaked cashews, plant-based milk, nutritional yeast, olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika (if using), salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.
  4. Heat the sauce: Transfer the blended sauce to a large saucepan over medium heat. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring frequently, until the sauce thickens slightly and is heated through. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  5. Combine pasta and sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the saucepan with the sauce. Stir well to coat the pasta evenly.
  6. Optional add-ins: If desired, mix in steamed broccoli, sautéed mushrooms, or other toppings.
  7. Serve: Divide the mac and cheese among plates or bowls. Garnish with fresh parsley, crispy vegan bacon bits, or a sprinkle of smoked paprika for extra flavor. Serve warm and enjoy!

  • Vitamins & Minerals: Nutritional yeast contributes vitamin B12 (if fortified) and other B vitamins.
